## Key Points

- Weiyao Wang, an 8-year Meta veteran who worked on multimodal systems including SAM3D, just left Meta to join AI startup Thinking Machines Lab (TML).
- TML recently signed a multibillion-dollar cloud deal with Google, gaining access to Nvidia's latest GB300 chips, putting them in the same infrastructure tier as Anthropic and Meta.
- A talent war is raging between Meta and TML - Meta has poached 7 of TML's founding members, while TML has been hiring significant talent from Meta.
- Notable Meta alumni at TML include Soumith Chintala (former co-founder of PyTorch and CTO), Piotr Dollár (co-author of Segment Anything), and several other research scientists and engineers.
- TML has also recruited talent from other companies including Waymo, OpenAI, Apple, Microsoft, and the buzzy coding startup Cognition.
- The startup currently has around 140 employees and is valued at $12 billion, creating financial upside that competes with Meta's well-known seven-figure pay packages without strings attached.
