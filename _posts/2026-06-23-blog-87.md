## Key Points

  Google is introducing new Gemini app features specifically designed for business owners
  Businesses can connect their Google Business Profile to Gemini with a single tap, giving the AI access to real-world context like customer reviews and performance data
  Gemini becomes an AI assistant that actually knows your business, allowing you to ask questions like "how did my business do this month?" and get analyzed insights
  The AI can help with review management by drafting tailored responses in your brand's voice and reference specific customer feedback
  Businesses can use Gemini to update operating hours, post seasonal updates, and identify profile gaps seamlessly
  Google is introducing Business notebooks as a centralized hub to organize chats, sources, Google Business Profile, and websites
  Business notebooks proactively surface key insights and recommend actionable steps, including alerts for unanswered customer questions or missing holiday hours
  The notebooks provide tailored recommendations for new market opportunities and operational optimizations based on local market data
  These features begin rolling out globally this month and aim to make AI more accessible for small business owners
  Google promises special Workspace and Gemini offers coming soon to help small businesses use AI tools for growth
