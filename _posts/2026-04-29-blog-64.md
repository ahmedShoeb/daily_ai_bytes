## Key Points

Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Meta are partnering to optimize Meta's AI software for AWS Graviton4 processors
Graviton4 processors are custom AWS chips optimized for large-scale cloud computing tasks like AI inference
The partnership brings together Meta's Llama 2 models with AWS's price-performance optimized hardware
Meta uses AWS as one of its strategic public cloud providers alongside its own on-premises infrastructure
The collaboration is making AWS Graviton4-based instances perform better when running Meta AI models, with more optimizations planned
AWS's Nitro System provides security benefits that appeal to enterprise customers deploying Meta AI models
The companies plan to increase Graviton4-based instance capacity to help organizations meet the growing demand for AI compute
This represents a convergence of leading AI models with specialized AI hardware in the cloud
