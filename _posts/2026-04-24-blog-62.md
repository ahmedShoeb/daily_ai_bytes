## Key Points

OpenAI has released ChatGPT Images 2.0, representing a new era of image generation
The release was announced on April 21, 2026 as an official company product release
Users can try the new image generation capabilities directly in ChatGPT
The release includes different image modes: Classic, Horizontal, Square, and Vertical formats
