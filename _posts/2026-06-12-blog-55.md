## Key Points

- Anthropic is launching Claude Fable 5, a new 'Mythos-class' model that's state-of-the-art on nearly all AI capability benchmarks and made safe for general use
- Fable 5 exceeds all previous Claude models in software engineering, knowledge work, vision, scientific research, and complex tasks - the more complex the task, the larger its advantage
- The model includes new safety classifiers that automatically redirect cybersecurity, biology/chemistry, and distillation queries to Claude Opus 4.8 instead of Fable 5 to prevent misuse
- Fable 5 includes 30-day data retention for business customers to help defend against novel attacks and jailbreaks, with strict privacy protections
- A separate model, Claude Mythos 5, is being launched for cyberdefenders and infrastructure providers with cybersecurity safeguards lifted, available through Project Glasswing collaboration with US government
- Mythos 5 has demonstrated groundbreaking capabilities in drug design (accelerating the process ~10x), novel scientific hypothesis generation, and genomics research
- Pricing for both models is $10 per million input tokens and $50 per million output tokens - less than half the price of Claude Mythos Preview
- Early customers report Fable 5 can compress months of software engineering into days, handles long-horizon coding tasks with unprecedented autonomy, and excels in finance and analytical tasks
- The model shows exceptional performance in vision tasks, including extracting numbers from scientific figures and rebuilding web app source code from screenshots alone
- Anthropic plans to expand access to Mythos 5 through a trusted access program for both cybersecurity organizations and biomedical researchers
