## Key Points

- Google I/O 2026 is scheduled for May 19-20 at Shoreline Amphitheater
- An anonymous Gemini Flash candidate has appeared on LM Arena showing competitive performance against Gemini 3.1 Pro
- Early impressions suggest Google may deliver flagship-grade reasoning in the cost-efficient Flash tier
- Vertex AI customers using Gemini 2 Flash are receiving migration notices to move to Gemini 3 Flash or 3.1 Flash-Lite
- A "Flash 3.2" model briefly appeared in the Gemini app model selector before being removed, signaling imminent release
- The upgrades would offer faster, sharper default responses without requiring Pro tier access
- Developers get a clean migration path off the 2.x family ahead of formal retirement
- Google hasn't commented publicly but the pattern suggests an announcement before I/O 2026
