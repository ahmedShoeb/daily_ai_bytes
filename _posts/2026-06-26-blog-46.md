## Key Points

- Momentic announces a major platform update, rebranding, and expanded accessibility for all developers
- The company addresses the growing gap between AI-accelerated coding and traditional QA testing capabilities
- Production incidents have increased nearly 58% since AI adoption accelerated, with 81% of enterprise leaders reporting more issues tied to AI-generated code
- New platform features include memory and knowledge base systems that learn your product's specific terminology, flows, and edge cases
- Coverage Agent automatically proposes new or updated tests by reading PR diffs and understanding what changed in the codebase
- Failure Classification Agent triages test failures to distinguish between real bugs, intentional changes, test issues, or transient errors
- New intent-based test format allows engineers to describe tests in plain English that both humans and AI agents can understand
- The platform now offers customizable quality standards - from pixel-perfect accuracy to ensuring users reach their destination
- Momentic has transitioned to a freemium model and is now open to all software engineering teams
- The company emphasizes autonomous, always-on testing systems that compound trust over time rather than manual testing workflows
