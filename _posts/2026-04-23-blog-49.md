## Key Points

- Meta developed the Effective Training Time (ETT%) metric to quantify training efficiency as the percentage of total end-to-end wall time dedicated to productive training
- The team achieved a major milestone by increasing ETT% to over 90% for offline training by the end of 2025
- Key optimization areas included Time to Start/Recover, checkpoint management, shutdown time optimizations, and failure reduction
- Communication optimizations removed unnecessary process group creations and communications between ranks, significantly improving sharding time
- Pipeline optimizations allowed PT2 compilation and data processing to run in parallel using 'fast batch' technology to accelerate initialization
- PT2 compilation time was reduced by approximately 40% through MegaCache implementation and dynamic shape handling improvements
- Async checkpointing and PyTorch native staging reduced GPU idle time during checkpoint saves by allowing background uploads while training continues
- Standalone model publishing strategy decoupled publishing from training, reducing shutdown time by approximately 30 minutes per job
- The team collaborated with infrastructure teams to develop new scheduling algorithms to reduce job preemption ratios
- Key optimizations have been open-sourced in PyTorch ecosystem components like TorchRec and PyTorch 2
