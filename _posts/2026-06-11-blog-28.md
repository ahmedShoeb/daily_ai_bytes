- Perplexity CEO Aravind Srinivas confirms plans for a 2028 IPO regardless of how Anthropic and OpenAI's public listings are received by the market
- Perplexity was already planning for a 2028 IPO timeline before the recent Anthropic and OpenAI filings
- Anthropic confidentially filed for an IPO last week and was last valued at nearly $1 trillion
- OpenAI also confidentially filed for an IPO on Monday
- The SpaceX IPO this week will serve as a leading indicator for how Anthropic and OpenAI's IPOs might perform
- Srinivas believes it's important for the AI industry that these mega-IPOs go well and thinks they will perform well
