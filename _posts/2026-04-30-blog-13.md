## Key Points

- Former DeepMind researcher David Silver raised a record $1.1 billion seed round for his startup Ineffable Intelligence
- The funding round values the months-old startup at $5.1 billion and is the largest ever seed round in Europe
- The round was co-led by Sequoia and Lightspeed with participation from Nvidia, DST Global, Index, Google, and the UK's Sovereign AI Fund
- Ineffable Intelligence focuses on reinforcement learning where AI models learn from experience rather than human data
- The company aims to 'make first contact with superintelligence' and create a 'superlearner that discovers all knowledge from its own experience'
- Founder David Silver was previously a UCL professor and lead of DeepMind's reinforcement learning team
- The announcement is part of a trend of Big Tech AI researchers leaving to launch their own startups with massive funding
- Recent similar startups include Recursive Superintelligence (founded by former DeepMind engineer) and AMI Labs (founded by former Meta AI chief Yann LeCun)
- UK Science and Technology secretary Liz Kendall called the investment supporting 'a company at the very frontier of AI' that could 'transform entire sectors'
