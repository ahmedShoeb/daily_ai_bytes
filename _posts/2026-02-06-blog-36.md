## Key Points

- Google Cloud is introducing new AI tools at NRF 2025 to help retailers operate more efficiently and personalize shopping experiences.
- **Google Agentspace** is a platform enabling retailers to build AI agents that automate tasks like product recommendations, real-time customer queries, and operational workflows such as inventory management and loss prevention.
- **Vertex AI Search for commerce** has been enhanced with advanced language models to improve product discovery for shoppers.
- **Connected Stores solution** integrates shoppers' devices with in-store systems to create a seamless omnichannel experience.
- Wayfair is using **Gemini on Vertex AI** to automate product tagging and categorization, speeding up product launches five times faster and saving hundreds of thousands of dollars annually.
- Wayfair also leverages **Gemini on Google Workspace** to boost employee collaboration and productivity.
- Google Cloud’s partnership with **NVIDIA** allows retailers to access AI Enterprise software, improving their ability to handle complex AI tasks at scale.
- **LiveX AI** and **Standard.ai** are using NVIDIA’s tools on Google Cloud to create AI-driven customer support and enhance in-store experiences through customer behavior analysis.
- **Everseen** is integrating its Vision AI platform with Google Cloud to enable real-time visual data processing in stores, improving theft detection, inventory management, and customer experience.
- Google Cloud’s partners, including **Shopify, Revionics, Bloomreach, and more**, are launching AI-powered tools to personalize marketing, optimize logistics, and enhance on-floor retail operations.
- These innovations aim to address challenges like rising costs, supply chain complexities, and sustainability while meeting evolving customer expectations.

## Key Topics Discussed

Alright, folks! If you're in the retail space, you're probably feeling the pressure to keep up with the rapid pace of change driven by AI. Google Cloud is stepping up big time at NRF 2025, Retail’s Big Show, with some seriously exciting new tools designed to help retailers meet these challenges head-on. They’re not just about keeping up—they’re about redefining what’s possible in retail with AI-powered solutions that enhance efficiency and personalization like never before.

First up, **Google Agentspace**—this is a game-changer! Agentspace is a platform that lets retailers build **AI agents**, which are like super-smart digital assistants. These agents can reason, plan, and remember, almost like having a mini AI team working for you. Imagine using AI to give your customers **real-time, personalized product recommendations**, answer their questions instantly, or even guide them through their entire shopping journey. But that’s just the tip of the iceberg. AI agents can also **automate inventory management, customer service, and loss prevention**, freeing your employees to focus on the stuff that really matters—like building relationships and tackling bigger challenges.

Now, let’s talk about **Vertex AI Search for commerce**. Google Cloud has just rolled out some major upgrades to this tool, using advanced language models to make product discovery smoother and more intuitive for shoppers. Whether someone is browsing online or in-store, they’ll find what they’re looking for faster and with way more accuracy.

And if you’re thinking about creating a **seamless omnichannel experience**, Google Cloud’s **Connected Stores solution** is here to bridge the gap between digital and physical shopping. It connects shoppers’ devices with in-store systems, making sure that every interaction—whether online or offline—feels cohesive and personalized.

Let’s dive into some real-world examples. **Wayfair**, the online home goods and furniture giant, is already leveraging Gemini on Vertex AI to revamp its product catalog. They’ve managed to **speed up product launches by five times** by automating tasks like tagging and categorization. This isn’t just about faster turnarounds—it’s also saving them **hundreds of thousands of dollars each year** while ensuring customers get a smoother, more accurate experience. Plus, Gemini is helping Wayfair **catch errors in product info and flag inappropriate content**, making their online platform safer for everyone. Wayfair is also using Gemini on Google Workspace to **boost employee productivity and collaboration**, creating a happier and more efficient workplace.

Over at **NVIDIA**, their partnership with Google Cloud is all about giving retailers the infrastructure they need to **run AI at scale**. With NVIDIA’s AI Enterprise software now available on Google Cloud, retailers can handle **more data and complex tasks** without worrying about system overloads. Companies like **LiveX AI** and **Standard.ai** are already seeing the benefits. LiveX AI is building AI agents that **cut down customer support costs** while delivering a more personalized experience. Meanwhile, Standard.ai is helping brick-and-mortar stores **understand customer behavior better** and improve the in-store experience—because nothing beats a tailored shopping journey!

Now, imagine walking into a store and having **AI-powered systems that instantly analyze what’s happening around you**. That’s exactly what **Everseen** is bringing to the table with their Vision AI platform, now integrated with Google Cloud’s distributed solutions. Retailers can process **vast amounts of visual data directly in-store**, providing real-time insights like **theft detection, optimized inventory management, and even personalized customer interactions**. It’s like having eyes everywhere, but with the brainpower of AI to make smarter decisions instantly.

But Google Cloud isn’t working alone. They’ve got a **powerhouse lineup of partners** who are also rolling out incredible AI tools for retailers. **Shopify, Revionics, LiveRamp, Revieve, and Eagle Eye** are all focused on **transforming marketing with AI**. Think personalized campaigns, deeper customer insights, and making every ad dollar count. And if you’re looking to **operate more sustainably**, companies like **Manhattan Associates, O9 Solutions, Impact Analytics, and 345 Global** are using AI to **optimize logistics, reduce waste, and minimize environmental impact**. It’s retail innovation with a side of sustainability—how cool is that?

Finally, for those of you on the retail floor, companies like **Vusion Group, NCR Voyix, and Zebra** are bringing AI to your operations. These tools help **personalize shopping experiences, streamline tasks, and make everything run smoother**. Whether you’re managing inventory, assisting customers, or just trying to keep up with the latest trends, AI is here to make your job easier and your customers happier.
