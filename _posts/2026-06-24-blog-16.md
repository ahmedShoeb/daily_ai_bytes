## Key Points

- Tech giants Microsoft, Google, Cisco, Nvidia, and Salesforce propose a new protocol called Agentic Resource Discovery (ARD)
- ARD aims to help AI agents discover and access tools needed to complete assigned tasks within enterprise environments
- The protocol addresses challenges in agentic AI implementation: determining which tools agents can use, where to find them, and how to use them safely
- ARD creates a standardized way for tools and services to be shared across different systems within corporate domains
- Example use case: An agent investigating a production problem may need to access engineering documentation, support tickets, deployment history, and observability systems managed by different registries
- ARD operates at two levels: Catalogs where organizations publish available capabilities, and Registries that act as search engines crawling those catalogs
- The ARD specification is now available with a quickstart guide for organizations to publish their own catalogs
- Organizations can join the community to participate in the evolution of the ARD protocol
