## Key Points

Anthropic announced the expansion of Project Glasswing, giving 150 partners across 15+ countries access to its powerful Mythos AI model
The Mythos AI model has proven highly effective at finding software vulnerabilities, with partners already revealing over 10,000 high or critical-level security flaws
New industries being added include power, water, healthcare, communications, and hardware sectors
This expansion comes just after Anthropic confidentially filed its IPO prospectus with the SEC, beating rival OpenAI to this milestone
The announcement follows Anthropic's Monday statement about offering access to the European Union
Experts have warned that Mythos's advanced cybersecurity capabilities could be misused by hackers to find vulnerabilities more quickly
The White House has held meetings with tech leaders and financial institutions to discuss safe deployment of advanced AI models like Mythos
Anthropic estimates that a major cyberattack could impact over 100 million people
Major Project Glasswing partners include Apple, Nvidia, Microsoft, CrowdStrike, and Palo Alto Networks
New companies joining include cloud data management platform Rubrik
