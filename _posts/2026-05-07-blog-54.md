## Key Points

- Presents an end-to-end training pipeline that jointly optimizes reconstruction and generation for image modeling
- Unlike previous two-stage approaches, this method combines tokenizer and generative model training in a single process
- Leverages vision foundation models to enhance 1D tokenizers for autoregressive modeling
- Achieves state-of-the-art FID score of 1.48 without guidance on ImageNet 256x256 generation
- Research focuses on autoregressive image modeling using visual tokenizers for compact latent representations
- Model enables direct supervision from generation results to the tokenizer
- Submitted as academic paper with revisions from May 1-4, 2026
