## Key Points

OpenAI is making fully automated agent-based AI researchers its North Star project, planning an autonomous AI intern by September and a full multi-agent research system for 2028
OpenAI plans to double its workforce to 8,000 employees by year-end, expanding across product, engineering, research, and sales with technical ambassadors for businesses
Elon Musk announced Terafab - a $20B joint venture between Tesla, SpaceX and xAI to build the world's largest chip facility in Austin, aiming for a terawatt of annual computing power
The software development landscape is shifting to software factories, with developers becoming factory billers and systems self-improving via user feedback and production data
MiniMax M2.7 offers 90% of Claude Opus 4.6's quality at 7% of the cost - $0.30/$1.20 per million tokens vs Claude's $5/$25
Cursor's new Composer 2 model appears to be based on Moonshot AI's Kimi 2.5, starting from an open base and trained with reinforcement learning
Tokenmaxxing is a new trend where AI-obsessed workers spend tokens to prove productivity, with some spending thousands monthly and a single agent consuming 700M tokens weekly
Flash-MoE runs 397B parameter models on MacBook Pro with 48GB RAM at 4.4+ tokens/second by streaming from SSD via custom Metal pipeline
OpenAI's data center spending and potential IPO have Wall Street concerned about the company's growth and spending approach
Tencent launched ClawBot to integrate WeChat with OpenClaw AI agent, advancing China's AI agent market with consumer and business applications
