## Key Points

Alibaba Cloud released HappyHorse 1.1, a major upgrade to its AI video generation model now ranked No. 2 globally across all Video Arena leaderboards
The model features multi-image reference capability (R2V) for maintaining consistent character identity across videos - a critical requirement for commercial production
HappyHorse uses a unified 15-billion-parameter Transformer architecture that processes text, image, video, and audio tokens in a single sequence
The AI video market has contracted dramatically with OpenAI discontinuing Sora due to financial unsustainability and ByteDance shelving Seedance 2.0 following copyright complaints
Alibaba is backing HappyHorse with a $52.7 billion global infrastructure buildout including new data centers in France and across Europe
The model is available on Alibaba Cloud Model Studio with a 40% launch discount, making it competitive against Google's Veo 3.1
HappyHorse 1.1 specifically addresses commercial pain points including facial artifacts, motion quality, audio-visual synchronization, and complex prompt handling
Alibaba faces geopolitical challenges as the Pentagon recently added the company to its list of Chinese military companies, creating additional complexity for Western enterprise adoption
The model supports text-to-video, image-to-video, subject-to-video, and video editing with integrated audio at no extra cost
Enterprise teams now have significantly fewer options for AI video generation following the collapse of Sora and freeze of Seedance
