## Key Points

Google is rolling out a new 'Thinking level' feature in the Gemini app that allows users to choose between 'Standard' and 'Extended' thinking modes for Fast (Gemini 3 Flash) and Gemini 3.1 Pro models
The feature is seeing limited rollout ahead of I/O 2026, similar to how Google AI Studio offers Low, Medium, and High thinking levels
Gemini app is preparing to add more third-party app integrations with Canva, Instacart, and OpenTable coming soon
Current third-party integrations include GitHub, OpenStax, Spotify, and WhatsApp
Canva integration will allow users to create designs, manage assets, collaborate on projects, and generate images that can be edited in Canva directly from Gemini prompts
Instacart integration enables grocery shopping by checking store availability, adding items to cart, and finding recipes with ingredients automatically added
OpenTable integration uses Reserve with Google to check restaurant availability, book/cancel reservations, and manage dining plans through Gemini prompts
Example use cases include creating wedding invitations in Canva, adding recipe ingredients to Instacart, and booking restaurant reservations through natural language commands
