## Key Points

- AI apps have reached 1.2 billion weekly active users, a 20x increase in two years - the fastest scaling app category in history
- ChatGPT alone accounts for 900 million WAUs (70% market share), rivaling major social platforms like TikTok and Instagram
- Gemini (Google's AI) is a distant second with 200-250M WAUs, while all other AI apps combined account for only ~10%
- ChatGPT reached 800-900M WAUs in roughly three years, faster than Instagram (5 years) and TikTok (4 years)
- Despite quarterly hype cycles featuring challengers like DeepSeek, XAI's Grok, and Gemini, only ChatGPT has maintained consistent growth
- The author's initial prediction that distribution incumbents Google and Meta would win AI was wrong - ChatGPT built its user base from scratch without existing platform advantages
- Consumer apps stratify into three tiers: Core Utilities (2-3B WAUs like WhatsApp), Social Platforms (1-1.5B WAUs like TikTok), and Niche apps (300-600M WAUs like Spotify)
- ChatGPT has moved from Niche tier toward Social tier, while Gemini is entering Niche tier
- Download charts show seasonal spikes for competitors, but only ChatGPT maintains steady momentum with both large installed base and high new downloads
- The author concludes ChatGPT is the only AI app on a path to becoming a "Core Utility" like WhatsApp or Chrome
