## Key Points

- This page collects architecture figures and fact sheets from several major LLM architecture comparison articles covering models from GPT-2 to recent open-weight LLMs
- The gallery includes over 30 different LLM architectures ranging from 270M parameters to 1 trillion parameters, showcasing the evolution from 2019 to 2026
- Featured models include foundational baselines like GPT-2 XL (1.5B) and Llama 3 (8B), along with modern architectures like DeepSeek V3 (671B) and Mistral Large 3 (673B)
- Different decoder types are represented: Dense architectures, Sparse MoE (Mixture of Experts), and Hybrid MoE designs
- Various attention mechanisms are showcased including MHA (Multi-Head Attention), GQA (Grouped Query Attention), MLA (Multi-Layer Attention), and linear attention hybrids
- The collection highlights key architectural innovations like RoPE (Rotary Positional Embeddings), QK-Norm, sliding-window attention, and various normalization schemes
- Context windows range from 1,024 tokens in older models to over 1,000,000 tokens in cutting-edge models like Llama 4 Maverick
- Licensing information is provided for each model, showing a trend toward more open licenses like Apache 2.0 and MIT
- The gallery serves as a visual reference companion to several technical articles analyzing LLM architectural advances
