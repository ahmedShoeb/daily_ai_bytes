## Key Points

- Engram is building AI that learns from users and deeply understands their work
- Current AI models don't truly understand user work as knowledge comes only from training data
- The company is introducing new technology to scale compute based on user context
- Engram aims to create AI that can learn from individual users' specific work environments and tasks
