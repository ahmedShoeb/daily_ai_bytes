## Key Points

- Meta is close to releasing its first new AI models after establishing a 'superintelligence' team led by former Scale.AI CEO Alexandr Wang
- Some of these new AI models will eventually be released with an open-source license
- Meta plans to focus on consumers rather than the enterprise market that OpenAI and Anthropic are targeting
- Meta is pursuing a hybrid strategy combining both proprietary and open-source models going forward
- The company had previously embraced open-source AI with its Llama models
- Mark Zuckerberg has declared open-source AI as 'the path forward' in a manifesto
- Meta previously delayed the launch of a new AI model due to performance issues
