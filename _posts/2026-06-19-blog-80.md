## Key Points

- NVIDIA's Blackwell platform achieved the fastest training times across all seven benchmarks in MLPerf Training 6.0
- The platform demonstrated the largest-scale training using 8,192 GPUs on NVIDIA Blackwell NVL72 systems
- NVIDIA was the only platform with submissions across all seven benchmarks, including two new mixture-of-experts (MoE) workloads: DeepSeek-V3 671B and GPT-OSS-20B
- NVIDIA submitted results on both GB200 NVL72 and GB300 NVL72 rack-scale systems, with GB300 delivering up to 1.6x faster training than GB200
- Microsoft Azure scaled Llama 3.1 405B training to 8,192 GPUs using GB200 NVL72 systems, achieving the fastest time to train at 7.07 minutes
- CoreWeave delivered the fastest time to train for DeepSeek-V3 671B at 2.02 minutes using GB300 NVL72 systems with Spectrum-X Ethernet
- The platform includes reliability features like self-healing capabilities, automatic fault routing, and NVIDIA Resiliency Extension (NVRx) for faster recovery
- 19 ecosystem partners participated in the benchmarks, including major cloud providers and hardware manufacturers
