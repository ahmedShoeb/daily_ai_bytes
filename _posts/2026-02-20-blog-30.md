## Key Points

- Cursor is launching a marketplace where AI agents can list and sell their workflows, allowing users to download and customize them for their needs
- Users can now create agents within Cursor and add them to their own 'agents bar' for easy access
- The marketplace will include both free and paid agent workflows, with paid agents enabling users to earn money from their creations
- Cursor provides a 'No-Code' interface that allows users to customize agents without writing code, making AI agent creation accessible to everyone
- The marketplace aims to create a flywheel effect where users can easily discover, use, and share useful AI agents
- Users can browse the marketplace by category, see usage data, and favorite agents they like
- There's a process for submitting agents to the marketplace where Cursor will review submissions for quality and appropriateness
- This launch marks a shift from Cursor being just an IDE (Integrated Development Environment) to a platform where AI agents can be created, shared, and monetized
