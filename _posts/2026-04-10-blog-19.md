## Key Points

- Anthropic added $10 billion in revenue in just the last month alone, which is twice Databricks' entire annual run rate
- Anthropic reached $10 billion in revenue in under 4 years, while other major tech companies like ServiceNow, Shopify, and Palo Alto Networks took 18-20 years to reach that milestone
- The article explores when Anthropic could become the world's most valuable company, surpassing NVIDIA
- NVIDIA currently has a $4.8 trillion market cap with $215 billion annual revenue and trades at 22x revenue
- To surpass NVIDIA, Anthropic would need about $200 billion in annual revenue
- Growth projections suggest Anthropic could overtake NVIDIA in: 3 years if growth continues at current pace, 4 years with steady deceleration, or 7 years with rapid normalization
- The article notes that Anthropic has significant customer concentration as a caveat
- Anthropic's journey from zero to $10 billion took just 42 months, then added another Databricks-equivalent in just 30 days
