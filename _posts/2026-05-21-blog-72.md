## Key Points

- Anthropic is acquiring Stainless, a leader in SDKs and MCP server tooling
- The acquisition aims to advance Claude's ability to connect to data and tools as AI shifts from models that answer to agents that act
- Stainless has powered the generation of every official Anthropic SDK since the earliest days of their API
- Founded in 2022, Stainless creates SDKs, CLIs, and MCP servers that let developers and agents use APIs
- The company generates SDKs across multiple programming languages including TypeScript, Python, Go, and Java
- Katelyn Lesse, Head of Platform Engineering at Anthropic, emphasized that "agents are only as useful as what they can connect to"
- Alex Rattray, Founder and CEO of Stainless, said the integration was an easy decision given what developers have built on Claude
- Anthropic created MCP (Model Context Protocol) to make agent connectivity possible
- The acquisition brings together the Stainless and Anthropic teams to push the frontier of developer experience and agent connectivity
