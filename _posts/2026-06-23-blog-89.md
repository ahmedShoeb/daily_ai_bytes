## Key Points

- MAI-Image-2.5 is Microsoft's strongest image model yet and ranks No. 2 on Arena's Image Edit leaderboard, surpassing competitors like Nano Banana 2.1
- The model achieves No. 3 ranking for text-to-image generation and No. 2 for image editing on Arena benchmarks
- Two versions are available: MAI-Image-2.5 for maximum fidelity and MAI-Image-2.5-Flash for fast, scalable production workloads
- Key features include enhanced text-to-image quality with better detail, complex visual reasoning for understanding scene structure, fine-grained edit control, and face/identity consistency across edits
- The model is already live in PowerPoint for presentation-ready image generation and rolling out to OneDrive for precise photo editing
- Pricing starts at $5 per 1M text tokens for the premium version and $1.75 per 1M tokens for the Flash version
- MAI-Image-2.5 includes layered safety guardrails with prompt and output filtering to detect harmful content
- The models are available to developers through Microsoft Foundry and OpenRouter platforms
