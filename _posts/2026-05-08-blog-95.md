## Key Points

- iOS 27 will give iPhone users choice in which AI models they want to use on-device
- Apple plans to allow users to pick from multiple third-party large language models
- The feature, called 'Extensions' internally, will let users access AI capabilities from installed apps through Apple Intelligence features
- Extensions will work with Siri, Writing Tools, Image Playground and other Apple Intelligence features
- The capability will also be available for iPadOS 27 and macOS 27
- Google and Anthropic models are currently being tested, while ChatGPT's status is less clear
- With Tim Cook stepping down, incoming CEO John Ternus must chart Apple's AI future
- Apple is perceived as 'behind' on AI compared to peers but still generates significant AI revenue
- Apple's strategy focuses on turning existing hardware into AI-centric experiences rather than building new AI infrastructure
