## Key Points

- Qwen-Image-2.0 is an omni-capable image generation foundation model that unifies high-fidelity generation and precise image editing within a single framework.
- The model addresses challenges in ultra-long text rendering, multilingual typography, high-resolution photorealism, robust instruction following, and efficient deployment.
- It uses Qwen3-VL as the condition encoder coupled with a Multimodal Diffusion Transformer for joint condition-target modeling.
- Supports instructions of up to 1K tokens for generating text-rich content like slides, posters, infographics, and comics.
- Significantly improves multilingual text fidelity and typography, and enhances photorealistic generation with richer details, realistic textures, and coherent lighting.
- Follows complex prompts more reliably across diverse styles through large-scale data curation and a customized multi-stage training pipeline.
- Extensive human evaluations show Qwen-Image-2.0 substantially outperforms previous Qwen-Image models in both generation and editing.
- Represents progress toward more general, reliable, and practical image generation foundation models.
