## Key Points

- Agentic AI is an advanced form of automation capable of achieving complex goals with limited human direction by simulating human-style decision-making
- Unlike generative AI that creates content from prompts, agentic AI orchestrates multiple subtasks to solve complex problems autonomously
- Agentic AI works alongside generative AI - for example, generating marketing copy and then automatically distributing it to targeted audiences
- Gartner predicts that by 2028, one-third of enterprise software applications will be agentic AI enabled, and 15% of daily work decisions will be made autonomously
- Agentic AI transforms search by understanding natural language queries and personalizing results based on user behavior and past interactions
- Good agentic AI systems are autonomous, proactive, integrable, specialized, adaptable, compliant, and not burdensome to implement
- Implementing agentic AI requires quality data, rigorous security protocols, ethical considerations to eliminate biases, and transparency about its use
- Agentic AI streamlines workflows and routine tasks, freeing human workers to focus on strategic planning rather than time-consuming administrative work
- The way people search is changing from keyword-based queries to natural language, with agentic AI understanding intent and micro-expressions of interest
- Organizations should partner with experts to implement agentic AI properly and capitalize on this major shift in digital operations
