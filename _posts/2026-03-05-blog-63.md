## Key Points

- Alibaba's Qwen Team released the Qwen3.5 Small Model Series with sizes ranging from 0.8B to 9B parameters
- The 9B model outperforms OpenAI's gpt-oss-120B (13.5x larger) on key benchmarks including multilingual knowledge and graduate-level reasoning
- Models use an Efficient Hybrid Architecture combining Gated Delta Networks with sparse Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) to overcome memory limitations
- The 9B model achieved 70.1 on MMMU-Pro visual reasoning, beating Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite (59.7) and Qwen3-VL-30B-A3B (63.0)
- On GPQA Diamond benchmark, the 9B model scored 81.7 vs. gpt-oss-120b's 80.1
- Models are natively multimodal with early fusion training, allowing sophisticated visual understanding previously requiring models 10x larger
- Available under Apache 2.0 license on Hugging Face and ModelScope for commercial use
- Small models (0.8B-2B) can run on phones, while 4B-9B models can run locally on laptops like M1 MacBook Air
- The series enables 'edge AI' applications including video analysis, document parsing, and autonomous coding with up to 1M context window
- Release comes during 'Agentic Realignment' era where local, efficient models enable autonomous agents without expensive cloud infrastructure
