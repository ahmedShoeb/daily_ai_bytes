## Key Points

Manus AI has launched the Manus Browser Operator, a new browser extension.
It's in beta for Pro, Plus, and Team users, supporting Chrome and Edge.
The extension enables Manus to operate within a user's local browser for automation.
It's designed for authenticated sessions on platforms like research and CRM systems.
This allows automation in trusted environments, bypassing cloud-only issues like logins or session expirations.
Users can activate the 'My Browser' connector and monitor workflows with full control.
It targets professionals and teams using premium data tools or complex web workflows.

## Key Topics Discussed

Hey everyone, we've got some exciting news from Manus AI! They've just rolled out their new Browser Operator extension, and it's currently in beta for all Pro, Plus, and Team users. This innovative tool is a game-changer because it brings Manus's powerful automation capabilities directly into your local browser. Think about it: you can now automate tasks within authenticated sessions on all those premium research platforms and CRM systems you rely on every day. Right now, it's rocking on Chrome and Edge, but Manus has plans to expand compatibility to even more browsers down the line. You'll be able to activate the 'My Browser' connector, authorize your secure sessions, and keep a close eye on your automated workflows in real-time, with full control and transparency over every single action. This is a huge win for professionals and teams who depend on premium data tools or complex web-based workflows that involve sensitive or authenticated information. One of the coolest things about the Manus Browser Operator is how it functions within your own trusted local environment. This means it cleverly bypasses those frustrating issues often found with cloud-only automation, like unfamiliar login prompts or session expirations that can totally derail your work. Unlike previous versions that were confined to a cloud sandbox, this extension gives you seamless access to resources behind logins and leverages your existing local setup for super reliable automation. Industry experts and early testers are already buzzing about the value of this hybrid approach, which perfectly bridges the gap between cloud and local browser automation, especially for tasks involving proprietary or subscription-based services. Manus, already well-known for their AI automation platform, is really aiming to solve those long-standing workflow bottlenecks by combining the best of isolated cloud automation with the familiarity and trust of local browser sessions. This is definitely one to watch if you're looking to streamline your online operations!
