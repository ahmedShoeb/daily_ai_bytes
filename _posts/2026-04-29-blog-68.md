## Key Points

Andon Market in San Francisco is billed as the world's first retail boutique run entirely by an artificial intelligence agent named Luna
The store is located on Union Street, a posh area known for yoga studios, jewelry stores, and cafes
The A.I. agent experiment comes from Andon Labs, which previously tested whether bots could run vending machines, radio stations, and household robots
Since opening on April 10, the store has been struggling and 'limping along' with various operational issues
The inventory appears random and disorganized, featuring two boxes of knockoff Connect Four games and four copies of a mushroom book
There's an excessive number of candles in all shapes, sizes, and smells throughout the store
The store lacks price tags, and when prices are disclosed, they seem unusually high even for San Francisco standards
The A.I. agent has struggled with employee scheduling and cannot stop ordering candles
The store front is bare with empty windows and no signs on the facade
