## Key Points

Anthropic launched Claude Tag, a persistent AI teammate that operates inside Slack channels as an always-on agent
Salesforce publicly promoted Claude Tag despite it competing directly with their own Slackbot and Agentforce platform
Some Salesforce employees are confused and anxious about the competitive overlap since Salesforce acquired Slack for $27.7 billion
Claude Tag allows users to type @Claude to assign tasks, breaks them into stages, and accumulates institutional knowledge over time
The situation creates an awkward positioning problem where Slack customers can choose between Slackbot, Agentforce, and Claude Tag
Salesforce has financial reasons to promote Claude Tag - spending $300M on Anthropic tokens this year and holding a 1% stake in Anthropic
Agentforce generated $800M in annual recurring revenue, up 169% year-over-year, potentially threatened by Claude Tag
Anthropic plans to expand Claude Tag to Microsoft Teams, email, and other tools, moving beyond Slack's ecosystem entirely
