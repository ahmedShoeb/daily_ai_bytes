## Key Points

- Microsoft's new image model MAI-Image-2.5 ranks third on the Arena text-to-image leaderboard
- This is the strongest image model yet from Microsoft, representing a significant quality improvement over the previous MAI-Image-2
- The model excels at text rendering, stylized illustration, and commercial imagery
- Major improvements include sharper text, better layouts, more coherent scenes, and polished brand-forward visuals
- The model shows strong visual reasoning across objects, scene structure, lighting, scale, and spatial relationships
- Particularly strong for professional creative work requiring detailed text and branding elements
- The model follows instructions closely and produces detailed images as intended
- Available now on Arena AI and coming to MAI Playground and Foundry within two weeks
- Continues the momentum from earlier MAI-Image-1 and MAI-Image-2 versions
