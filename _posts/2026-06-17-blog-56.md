## Key Points

-Publication of first AgentPerf results, the industry's first agentic AI benchmark from Artificial Analysis
-NVIDIA Blackwell Ultra NVL72 platform shows 20x more agents per megawatt than NVIDIA Hopper systems
-Agentic AI differs fundamentally from conversational AI, involving dozens to hundreds of chained LLM calls with tool execution
-Existing AI inference benchmarks weren't designed for agentic workloads with complex chained calls and growing context
-AgentPerf measures performance using real coding agent trajectories from public repositories across 12+ programming languages
-Benchmark measures how many concurrent agentic tasks a platform can handle while meeting responsiveness thresholds
-NVIDIA's performance advantage comes from full-stack codesign including CUDA kernels and TensorRT LLM optimizations
-Leading inference providers including Baseten, DeepInfra and Together AI already serve agentic workloads on NVIDIA Blackwell
-Together AI powers Cursor's AI coding platform and DeepInfra powers Pam.ai's AI workforce platform for car dealerships on Blackwell
