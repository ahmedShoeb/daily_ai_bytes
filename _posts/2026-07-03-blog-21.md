## Key Points

Anthropic is launching an internal drug discovery program called Claude Science as part of its push into healthcare AI
The company will focus on discovering treatments for 'neglected' diseases that traditional pharmaceutical companies typically avoid
Anthropic's life sciences head Eric Kauderer-Abrams announced the program at an event in San Francisco
The company believes it needs to have direct experience in drug discovery to build effective AI tools for the industry
As a public benefit company, Anthropic says it can choose programs based on patient benefit rather than commercial potential
The company plans to work alongside drugmakers it's trying to court for its Claude Science product
Anthropic hasn't specified what it will do with any promising drug candidates it discovers
Tech giants like Alphabet, Apple, and Amazon have previously entered healthcare with mixed results
The move represents Anthropic's latest effort to crack the healthcare AI market
Anthropic will share more details as the work progresses
