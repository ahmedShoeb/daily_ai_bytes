## Key Points

- Anthropic confidentially submitted a draft registration statement on Form S-1 to the SEC
- This filing is for a proposed initial public offering (IPO) of Anthropic's common stock
- The confidential submission gives Anthropic the option to go public after SEC review
- The actual IPO will depend on market conditions and other factors
- The number of shares and price range have not been determined yet
- This announcement is made under Rule 135 of the Securities Act of 1933
- The filing is not an offer to sell securities, only a preliminary step in the IPO process
- Any actual securities offering will only happen after proper registration with the SEC
