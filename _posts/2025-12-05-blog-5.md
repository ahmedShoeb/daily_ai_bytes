## Key Points

Amp is spinning out of Sourcegraph to become an independent research lab.
The goal is to let software builders harness the full power of artificial intelligence.
Amp believes the way software is developed will change fundamentally and drastically.
The company focuses on shipping products rather than just writing papers.
Amp Inc. provides more freedom to explore the frontier of AI.
Amp is currently profitable, which led to its spin-out from Sourcegraph.

## Key Topics Discussed

Amp is officially spinning out of Sourcegraph to establish itself as an independent research lab named Amp Inc. The core mission of this new entity is to empower software developers to fully leverage the capabilities of artificial intelligence. Amp's founders believe that the landscape of software development is on the brink of fundamental and drastic change, and they are committed to actively shaping this future. Unlike traditional research approaches, Amp Inc. aims to deliver tangible products rather than just theoretical papers, putting innovations directly into the hands of users. This move to an independent company grants Amp greater autonomy to relentlessly pursue cutting-edge AI developments and explore unconventional possibilities. Notably, Amp is already a profitable venture, a testament to its successful traction, which ultimately paved the way for this spin-out from Sourcegraph. The company extends an invitation for others to join them in exploring the vast potential of AI.
