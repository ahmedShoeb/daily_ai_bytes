## Key Points

- OpenAI is launching a personal finance feature preview for ChatGPT Pro users in the U.S.
- Users can securely connect financial accounts from over 12,000 institutions via Plaid integration
- ChatGPT provides a dashboard showing portfolio performance, spending, subscriptions, and upcoming payments
- The system uses GPT-5.5 Thinking for complex financial reasoning based on user's financial context and goals
- Users can share financial context like savings goals or debts that ChatGPT saves as 'financial memories'
- The feature respects user privacy - ChatGPT can't see full account numbers or make changes to accounts
- Users can disconnect accounts anytime, which deletes synced data from OpenAI's systems within 30 days
- OpenAI worked with over 50 finance professionals to develop and evaluate the feature
- The vision is to help users take action beyond just answering questions, with plans to integrate with partners like Intuit
- More than 200 million people already use ChatGPT monthly for financial planning and budgeting questions
