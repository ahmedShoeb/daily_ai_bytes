## Key Points

- Software has shifted from packaged tools to embedded intelligence, where products perform work rather than just supporting it
- Traditional pricing models (licenses, seats, subscriptions) are no longer adequate for AI-driven software that delivers continuous, dynamic value
- Value has transformed from the Software Era (features/licenses) to Access Era (subscriptions) to the current Value Era (outcome-based pricing)
- Modern pricing complexity includes usage-based metrics, agent-based pricing, workflow-based pricing, and hybrid models running in parallel
- The combination of product-led growth (PLG) and sales-led growth (SLG) creates exponential monetization complexity
- Companies need a new monetization operating model with three critical properties: predictability, visibility, and control
- The operating model requires clear ownership (like a Chief Value Officer), structured processes, and specialized tools
- Metronome provides a platform that enables self-serve pricing, safe experimentation, systematized rate cards, and direct connection between value measurement and billing
- The operating model must support 11 key capabilities including version control, unified data platforms, and cross-functional alignment
- Successful value monetization requires treating pricing as a product with dedicated ownership, telemetry, and structured change processes
