## Key Points

- Google is expanding its Personal Intelligence feature to all users in the U.S., previously only available to paid users
- Personal Intelligence allows Google's AI assistant to tailor responses by connecting across your Google ecosystem (Gmail, Google Photos, etc.)
- The feature is available in AI Mode in Search, the Gemini app, and Gemini in Chrome
- Personal Intelligence is off by default - users must opt-in to connect their Google apps
- Examples include: suggesting all-weather tires after recognizing family road-trip photos, or creating tailored vacation itineraries based on hotel bookings and past travel photos
- Gemini doesn't train directly on your Gmail or Photos - it only trains on specific prompts and model responses
- Currently only available for personal Google accounts, not for Workspace business, enterprise, or education users
- The expansion to free-tier users in the U.S. is starting to roll out now
