## Key Points

- OpenAI has moved GPT-5.6 into a narrow preview with vetted partners through Codex and the API
- The GPT-5.6 family splits into three tiers: Sol (flagship), Terra (mid-cost), and Luna (fastest/cheapest)
- New features include a reworked reasoning-effort control rendered as a slider rather than preset buttons
- A new 'max' setting gives Sol more capacity for long problems, plus 'ultra' mode for heavier jobs
- Timing coincides with Anthropic's Fable 5 moving to usage-based credits on July 7
- The Sol, Terra, and Luna labels represent a shift toward durable capability tiers that advance on separate schedules
- Broad access depends on US government cybersecurity review approvals rather than a fixed calendar
- Real-time voice features present in earlier builds appear to have been removed from the current app
