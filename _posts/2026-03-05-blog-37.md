## Key Points

- Anthropic experienced widespread disruptions affecting thousands of users accessing Claude services
- The outage impacted Claude.ai and Claude Code, though the Claude API remained functional
- Most users encountered errors when attempting to log in to the services
- Anthropic has identified the issue and is implementing a fix, though hasn't detailed the root cause
- The disruption follows a surge in Claude's popularity, with the app rising to the top of App Store charts
- Claude recently overtook rival ChatGPT in app store rankings after spending time below the top 20
- The user influx may be related to attention around Anthropic's negotiations with the Pentagon
- U.S. President Donald Trump ordered federal agencies to stop using Anthropic products
- The dispute involves safeguards preventing Department of Defense use for mass domestic surveillance or autonomous weapons
- Secretary of Defense Pete Hegseth plans to designate Anthropic as a supply-chain threat
