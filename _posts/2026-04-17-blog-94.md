## Key Points

- Agentic AI is an advanced form of automation that achieves complex goals with minimal human direction, using ML models to simulate human-style decision-making
- Unlike generative AI which creates content from prompts, agentic AI orchestrates AI agents to execute actions and achieve specific goals automatically
- Agentic AI can handle complex queries like finding products based on descriptive prompts (e.g., 'sunglasses like Jack Black wore in his latest movie')
- Gartner predicts that one-third of enterprise software applications will be agentic AI enabled by 2028, with 15% of daily work decisions made autonomously
- Agentic AI enhances traditional search by understanding natural language queries and providing more relevant, personalized results
- Good agentic AI should be autonomous, proactive, integrable, specialized, adaptable, compliant, and easy to implement
- Integration requires accurate data, thorough security policies, and consideration of ethical implications and bias elimination
- Agentic AI streamlines workflows, increases efficiency, and frees up human resources for strategic planning rather than replacing human contributions
