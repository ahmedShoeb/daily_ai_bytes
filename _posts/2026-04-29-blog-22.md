## Key Points

- Cohere and Aleph Alpha are forming a transatlantic alliance to provide enterprise-grade sovereign AI alternative
- The partnership combines Cohere's global AI scale with Aleph Alpha's research excellence and deep institutional relationships
- Targets the $600B sovereign AI market segment out of a projected $1 trillion annual AI services market
- Aims to accelerate development of next-generation frontier models with top-tier talent from Canada and Germany
- Designed to serve highly-regulated sectors including public sector, finance, defense, energy, manufacturing, telecom, and healthcare
- Schwarz Group (parent company of STACKIT cloud service) intends to lead $600M Series E funding round
- Partnership emphasizes European sovereignty, transparency, and regulatory compliance for AI systems
- Combined entity will partner with Schwarz Group's STACKIT cloud service for sovereign AI deployments
- Organizations will maintain absolute control over their data with this 'sovereign-first' approach
- Positions as a counterweight to single-vendor dependency in the increasingly concentrated AI market
