## Key Points

- Google is reviving efforts to add a projects feature to Gemini, with testing found in Gemini Enterprise rather than the consumer app
- The Enterprise version will have a dedicated projects section for organizing chats by topic and uploading files for cross-chat reference
- Users will be able to set specific goals that guide how Gemini responds to their queries
- This functionality mirrors features already offered by Anthropic (Claude Projects) and OpenAI (ChatGPT organizational tools)
- The feature has been heavily requested by users, making Google's delayed implementation surprising
- Gemini Enterprise also now includes starter prompts to help onboard workplace users with practical use cases
- Gemini 3.1 Pro is already available in the model selector with improved reasoning capabilities
- Upcoming features include a notifications button for completed scheduled tasks and an agents section tab to aggregate all runs and executions
- Google appears to be expanding Gemini Enterprise capabilities at a measured pace compared to competitors
- The upcoming Google I/O conference could be where Google makes a bigger push in the enterprise AI space
