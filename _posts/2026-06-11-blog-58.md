## Key Points

- Apple announced the 'Siri AI' voice assistant update at its Worldwide Developers Conference keynote
- The new Siri AI will launch 'this fall' alongside OS updates and feature tighter integration across Apple's operating systems
- Siri AI is powered by a new Google-enhanced on-device Foundation Models update
- Apple's approach focuses on user-centered AI rather than racing forward for the sake of AI technology
- The update enables multi-conversation capabilities beyond one-shot tasks for a 'brand new conversational experience'
- Demo examples showed Siri AI handling complex, multi-step conversations involving schedule information, recipes, and app integration
- In one demo, Siri helped plan a watch party menu by combining World Cup schedule info, recipes, and friend recommendations from Messages
- Another demo showed Siri solving a multi-step navigation problem involving photo location identification and friend address lookup
- The new Siri AI represents Apple's long-delayed 'Apple Intelligence' update that has been anticipated for some time
