## Key Points

- This is a GitHub repository containing a curated collection of research papers on generative information retrieval (GenIR)
- The repository supports a survey titled 'From Matching to Generation: A Survey on Generative Information Retrieval'
- Generative information retrieval represents a paradigm shift from traditional matching-based methods to generative approaches
- The survey divides GenIR research into two main categories: generative document retrieval and reliable response generation
- Generative document retrieval involves retrieval by directly generating document identifiers without relying on large-scale indexing
- Reliable response generation uses language models to generate user-centric and reliable responses that directly meet users' information needs
- The repository contains organized research papers covering topics like evaluation methods, challenges, and future prospects in GenIR
- Key research areas include Differentiable Search Index (DSI), dynamic retrievers, neural corpus indexers, and various other generative retrieval approaches
- The project aims to provide a comprehensive reference for researchers in the GenIR field and encourage further development
