## Key Points

i 1. Anthropic has introduced Claude Science, a new AI workbench specifically designed for scientific researchers
i 2. The platform integrates over 60 curated tools and packages for genomics, single-cell analysis, proteomics, structural biology, cheminformatics, and more
i 3. Claude Science generates fully reproducible scientific artifacts with auditable histories, including figures, manuscripts, and the exact code that produced them
i 4. It manages computing resources automatically, scaling from a single GPU to hundreds on-demand while keeping sensitive data on lab infrastructure
i 5. The platform uses specialist agents that can query hundreds of scientific databases and journals simultaneously, synthesizing information across multiple sources
i 6. Beta users have already used Claude Science for single-cell RNA sequencing, CRISPR screen design, protein structure prediction, and cheminformatics tasks
i 7. Real-world examples show significant time savings - one neuroscientist's review writing process went from 2 years to accelerated completion with AI assistance
i 8. Claude Science is now available in beta for Claude Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise users on macOS and Linux
i 9. Anthropic is offering $30,000 in credits to support up to 50 AI for Science projects, with applications open through July 2026
i 10. The platform maintains data security by running on researchers' own infrastructure, ensuring sensitive datasets never leave lab systems
