## Key Points

Meta has launched Muse Spark, the foundational model now powering Meta AI across all Meta apps and devices
The rollout introduces faster voice responses, natural conversation capabilities with topic/language switching mid-discussion
New features include real-time visual recognition through device cameras for contextual understanding of objects and landmarks
Shopping mode aggregates Facebook Marketplace and internet listings with map-based browsing and advanced filters
Initial availability targets US and Canada users, with gradual expansion to Ray-Ban Meta and Oakley Meta glasses
Muse Spark is designed as a compact, fast model with advanced reasoning in science, math, health, and visual coding
The model enables real-world contextual understanding and multitasking through subagents, distinguishing it from previous models
Live AI features allow users to point cameras at objects for immediate context and assistance
Meta Superintelligence Labs rebuilt the AI stack for faster, more capable models with safety and privacy safeguards
This represents a step toward more contextual personal assistants with eventual goal of achieving personal superintelligence
