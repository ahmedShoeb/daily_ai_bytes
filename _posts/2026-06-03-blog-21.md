## Key Points

- AI-generated traffic now makes up 70% of all internet traffic, with automated, malicious bots being the primary driver of this growth
- AI-generated traffic increased by 200% in 2025 compared to the previous year
- Humans are no longer the primary consumers of online services and information
- Three major categories of AI-driven traffic were identified: Autonomous AI, Adversarial AI (threats), and Beneficial AI
- Malicious AI bots are now the biggest threat to enterprises, targeting API endpoints and app logic rather than just vulnerabilities
- The report warns that current AI-driven traffic will fundamentally transform cybersecurity and how enterprises consume technology
- The security ecosystem must evolve its detection and defense to handle AI's impact on threats and traffic
