## Key Points

- Anthropic is working with chemists to enhance Claude's chemistry capabilities, starting with NMR spectrum analysis
- Claude Opus 4.7 performs comparably or better than specialized chemistry software ChemDraw and MestReNova on NMR predictions
- For 1D NMR hydrogen predictions, Opus 4.7 achieved an average error of ±0.079 ppm—less than half the tolerance window chemists consider correct
- Claude successfully performs inverse structure elucidation—proposing molecular structures from experimental NMR spectra and molecular formulas
- Claude successfully identified all 8 simpler molecular structures from spectra and formula alone, and correctly identified harder structures with additional hints
- The model shows particular strength in predicting NMR peak splitting patterns and spacing, outperforming dedicated chemistry software
