## Key Points

Anthropic plans to spend $200 billion on Google Cloud services over the next five years, according to a report from The Information.
This $200 billion commitment would represent more than 40% of Google's $462 billion backlog as of Q1 2024, which nearly doubled from $240 billion in Q4 2023.
Google shares gained in post-market trading following the news of this massive cloud spending commitment.
The relationship between Google and Anthropic has been deepening, with Google reportedly planning to invest up to $40 billion in Anthropic.
Unlike Oracle's experience with OpenAI (where concentration risk hurt Oracle's stock), Google appears to be on more secure financial footing with Anthropic as a customer.
Anthropic has been aggressively securing compute capacity due to the popularity of Claude Code and Cowork causing compute constraints and user frustration.
Anthropic has expanded deals with multiple providers including CoreWeave, Amazon, Google, and Broadcom to address compute shortages.
The report suggests Anthropic may be viewed as a more reliable customer than OpenAI in the AI infrastructure market.
