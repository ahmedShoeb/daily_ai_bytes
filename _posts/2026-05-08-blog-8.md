## Key Points

- Google is partnering with XPRIZE and Range Media Partners through its 100 ZEROS initiative to support the Future Vision XPRIZE
- The competition offers a $3.5 million global film competition seeking optimistic, technology-forward visions of the future
- Google will serve as the creative technology partner and work with the grand prize winner to turn their submission into a full-length feature film
- The competition encourages the use of AI tools like Google Flow alongside traditional filmmaking methods
- Submissions are open through August 15, 2026 for short films and trailers
- The goal is to lower production barriers and augment the creative process for emerging filmmakers
- Entries can use traditional live-action, animation, or AI tools to envision positive technological futures
- The initiative aims to inspire the next generation through storytelling about optimistic futures
