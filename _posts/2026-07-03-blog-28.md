## Key Points

- OpenAI engineers cut inference costs by more than half for guest ChatGPT users earlier this month
- The number of Nvidia GPUs needed to serve guest users dropped to just a few hundred
- Guest users only have access to limited ChatGPT features, so it's unclear if these cost savings apply to full product users
- Deepseek recently released an open-source method that can speed up inference requests by 60 to 85 percent
- These efficiency gains could free up resources for scaling services, better models, faster responses, or higher margins
- Data center buildouts are moving slowly, so these gains provide breathing room rather than reducing chip demand
