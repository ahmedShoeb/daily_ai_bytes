## Key Points

OpenAI is quietly testing a next-generation image generation model internally referred to as Image V2
The model surfaced on LM Arena in three distinct variants: packingtape-alpha, maskingtape-alpha, and gaffertape-alpha
Some ChatGPT users have gained permanent access to the new model, while others see it through A/B testing
Early impressions suggest Image V2 can render realistic UI interfaces with correctly spelled button text, addressing a longstanding weakness in AI image generators
The model shows strong prompt adherence and compositional understanding
Comparisons to Google's Nano Banana Pro are circulating, with Image V2 competitive in areas where OpenAI's current model trails
OpenAI is operating under a 'code red' posture since Google's Gemini 3 and Nano Banana Pro began eating into its market position in late 2025
The key question is whether OpenAI will maintain the model's current quality at launch or dial it back for cost and safety reasons
Pricing will matter considerably as GPT Image 1.5 already undercuts its predecessor by 20% on API costs
No official announcement yet, with A/B testing phase lasting from days to weeks
