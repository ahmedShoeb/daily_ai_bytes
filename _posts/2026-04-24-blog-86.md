## Key Points

- Google has introduced two new evolutions of its autonomous research agent: Deep Research and Deep Research Max
- Deep Research Max represents a significant performance leap across industry-standard benchmarks for retrieval and reasoning capabilities
- The agents now integrate Gemini 3.1 Pro, transforming from summarization engines into foundations for enterprise workflows
- Deep Research can now search the web, arbitrary remote MCPs, file uploads, and connected file stores simultaneously
- The agents can natively generate high-quality charts and infographics, not just text
- Deep Research supports collaborative planning, allowing users to review and refine research plans before execution
- Real-time streaming capabilities let users track intermediate reasoning steps and receive outputs as they're generated
- Google is collaborating with companies like FactSet, S&P Global, and PitchBook on MCP server designs for financial data integration
- Deep Research Max delivers significantly more comprehensive reports than the December 2024 release, consulting more sources and identifying critical nuances
- The agents are available starting today in public preview via paid tiers in the Gemini API
