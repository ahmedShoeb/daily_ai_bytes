## Key Points

- Anthropic has launched Claude Fable 5, a Mythos-class model with state-of-the-art performance across software engineering, knowledge work, vision, scientific research, and other areas
- For cybersecurity defense purposes, Anthropic also launched Claude Mythos 5, the same underlying model as Fable 5 but with some safeguards lifted for trusted partners
- Fable 5 includes new safety classifiers that automatically redirect sensitive requests about cybersecurity, biology/chemistry, and model distillation to Claude Opus 4.8
- The models cost $10 per million input tokens and $50 per million output tokens - less than half the price of Claude Mythos Preview
- Fable 5 is initially available free on subscription plans until June 22, 2026, after which users will need usage credits
- Early testing showed Fable 5 compressing months of engineering work into days at Stripe, and Mythos 5 accelerated drug design by around 10x
- The models demonstrate exceptional performance in areas like complex coding tasks, financial analysis, vision tasks, and scientific hypothesis generation
- Anthropic has implemented a 30-day data retention policy for Mythos-class models to help defend against sophisticated attacks while protecting privacy
- Trusted access programs are being developed for cybersecurity organizations and biomedical researchers to safely leverage the models' advanced capabilities
