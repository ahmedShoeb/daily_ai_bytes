## Key Points

- Meta is reportedly training an AI avatar based on Mark Zuckerberg's image, voice, mannerisms, tone, and public statements
- The AI clone would allow employees to interact with Zuckerberg's digital version to feel more connected to the founder
- Zuckerberg himself is involved in training the AI avatar
- If successful, Meta may expand this feature to allow creators to make AI avatars of themselves
- Meta previously showed a live demo of AI personas for creators and allows AI versions for Instagram comment interactions
- Zuckerberg also reportedly spends 5-10 hours per week coding on Meta's AI projects and participating in technical reviews
- Separately, Zuckerberg is creating an AI agent to help him complete personal tasks
