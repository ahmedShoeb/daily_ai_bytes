## Key Points

- NVIDIA's first-ever Vera CPU units have been delivered to Anthropic, OpenAI, SpaceXAI, and Oracle Cloud Infrastructure
- The Vera CPU is NVIDIA's first custom CPU specifically designed for the age of agentic AI
- NVIDIA VP Ian Buck hand-delivered the CPUs, including one delivery to SpaceXAI's Elon Musk
- Vera features 88 custom NVIDIA-designed Olympus cores with 1.2TB/s memory bandwidth and 50% faster per-core performance
- Oracle Cloud Infrastructure plans to deploy hundreds of thousands of Vera CPUs starting in 2026
- OCI becomes the first cloud provider to deploy Vera at hyperscale for enterprise customers
- The CPU is designed to handle the unique demands of agentic AI workloads like orchestration, control, and data movement
- Vera pairs with NVIDIA Rubin GPUs via NVLink-C2C, sharing unified memory architecture for 2x energy efficiency
- The CPU addresses the increasing demand for CPUs in AI workloads beyond just GPU acceleration
- Vera is part of NVIDIA's larger ecosystem including Rubin GPU, BlueField-4 DPU, Spectrum-X and MGX rack architecture
