## Key Points

- Cursor is introducing significant changes to their Teams pricing structure, including increased usage limits and a new Premium seat tier
- Every Teams seat now has two separate usage pools: one for Cursor's first-party models (Composer and Auto) and another for third-party APIs
- The new Premium seat offers 5x the usage of the Standard seat at only 3x the cost, designed for power users who drive most team spending
- Standard seats now include more total usage at the same price: $32/month (annual) or $40/month (monthly)
- Premium seats are priced at $96/month (annual) or $120/month (monthly)
- The dashboard now provides real-time usage visibility with split tracking between Auto+Composer and third-party models
- New smart alerting system allows admins to configure dollar-based alerts delivered via Slack or email
- Changes take effect immediately for new customers and for renewing customers starting July 1st, 2026
- The company sees these changes as improving cost predictability and helping teams control spending
