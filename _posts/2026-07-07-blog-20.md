## Key Points

- ZCode is the official development environment for GLM-5.2
- GLM Coding Plan subscribers get 1.5x usage quota in ZCode
- BYOK (Bring Your Own Keys) supported - works with existing subscriptions and APIs
- Available on macOS, Windows, and Linux
- Download available at zcode.z.ai/en
