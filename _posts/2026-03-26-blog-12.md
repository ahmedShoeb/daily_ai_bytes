## Key Points

- DynaEdit is a training-free video editing method that enables complex video modifications using pretrained text-to-video flow models
- The method addresses challenges in editing dynamic events and object interactions in videos
- It overcomes limitations of existing approaches that struggle with low-frequency misalignment and high-frequency jitter
- DynaEdit enables dynamic object insertion where new objects interact with existing scene elements
- The method supports object swapping with scene adaptation and interaction capabilities
- It allows modification of actions performed by entities within the video scene
- Global effects like lighting and atmospheric changes can be applied to entire scenes
- The approach is model-agnostic, using an inversion-free method that doesn't intervene in model internals
- DynaEdit represents state-of-the-art results for complex text-based video editing tasks
