## Key Points

WorldStereo is a new framework that connects camera-guided video generation with 3D scene reconstruction through geometric memory modules
It addresses the challenge of reconstructing consistent 3D scenes from generated videos, which current methods struggle with due to limited camera control and inconsistent content across different camera views
The system features two key memory modules: global-geometric memory for precise camera control and coarse structural priors, and spatial-stereo memory for focusing on fine-grained details using 3D correspondence
These modules enable WorldStereo to generate multi-view consistent videos under precise camera control, which facilitates high-quality 3D reconstruction
WorldStereo shows impressive efficiency by benefiting from distribution matching distilled VDM backbone without requiring joint training
The framework functions as a powerful world model capable of tackling diverse scene generation tasks from both perspective and panoramic images with high-fidelity 3D results
Extensive experiments demonstrate effectiveness across both camera-guided video generation and 3D reconstruction benchmarks
Models will be released to the public
