## Key Points

- Ex-Tesla board member David Sacks is drafting AI policy ideas for a potential Trump return
- Sacks proposes scrapping current AI safeguards and promoting 'open source' development
- Plan would end Biden-era rule requiring AI companies to share safety results with government
- Would halt efforts to monitor AI chip sales to China
