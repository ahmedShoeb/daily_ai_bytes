## Key Points

This repository tracks the transition from Modular Assembly to Native Multimodal Modeling (NMM) where multiple modalities are intrinsically integrated into a unified framework
The NMM ecosystem is categorized by three paradigms: Multi-to-Text (M2T), Multi-to-Target (M2G), and Multi-to-Multi (M2M)
The list includes foundational models like LLaVA and CogVLM that maintain modality-aware boundaries
Massive state-of-the-art mid-fusion architectures include Qwen2.5-VL, Qwen3-VL, and InternVL-3.5
Scale-driven industrial implementations feature GLM-4.5V, Kimi K2, and NVIDIA's Nemotron 3 Nano Omni
The repository includes direct synthesis models that bypass traditional decoders for photorealistic video and speech generation
Omni-directional unified space models include Chameleon, AnyGPT, Moshi, and Emu3 that treat all modalities equivalently
The resource is a companion to the paper 'Toward Native Multimodal Modeling: A Roadmap' published in 2026
The curated list serves as a comprehensive model zoo and reading list for born-native multimodal foundation models
The taxonomy helps track the latest landmark works and welcomes community contributions via GitHub issues and pull requests
