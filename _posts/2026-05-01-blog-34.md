## Key Points

- ElevenLabs has introduced Agent Templates on its ElevenAgents platform for faster deployment of AI agents
- Templates provide pre-built conversational agent frameworks addressing common business needs
- Available use cases include customer support, onboarding, sales, feedback collection, and front desk operations
- Each template comes with predefined system prompts, workflows, and integration scaffolding
- The release is available to all ElevenLabs users through the ElevenAgents dashboard
- Targeted at teams responsible for customer experience, internal training, sales, and reception
- Features include adaptable conversation flows and logic for specific scenarios like lead qualification
- Templates significantly reduce setup time and minimize trial-and-error phases for production-ready agents
- ElevenLabs offers a broader selection of templates with detailed workflows compared to some competitors
- Early enterprise feedback highlights flexibility and reduced ramp-up time for live deployments
