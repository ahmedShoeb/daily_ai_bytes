## Key Points

- The paper investigates the benefits of using Spiking, ReLU, and quantization for efficient LLM deployment
- Researchers explore how these activation functions and quantization techniques can optimize large language model performance on resource-constrained devices
- The study provides comparative analysis between different activation strategies and their impact on model efficiency and accuracy
- Findings suggest practical approaches for reducing computational overhead while maintaining model quality in real-world deployment scenarios
- The work contributes to the broader field of efficient AI system design for edge computing and mobile applications
