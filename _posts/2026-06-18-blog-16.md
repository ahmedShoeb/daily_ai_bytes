## Key Points

- AWS WAF now includes AI traffic monetization capability that lets content owners charge AI bots for accessing protected web content
- This capability helps content owners set per-request pricing by content path, bot category, or verification tier without modifying infrastructure
- AI bot traffic now accounts for over 50% of web traffic for many content providers, growing more than 300% year-over-year
- Unlike traditional crawlers that send referral traffic, AI bots consume content without returning measurable traffic back to original sources
- Content owners can define granular access policies per agent type and collect payments in stablecoins to their preferred wallet
- AWS WAF Bot Control classifies over 650 distinct AI bot types including GPTBot, Claude-Web, and Perplexity-Bot
- The system uses verification tiers (Verified vs Unverified) based on cryptographic signatures or behavioral fingerprinting
- Payment settlement is provided by Coinbase's x402 Facilitator with Stripe integration coming soon
- AI traffic monetization is available now for Amazon CloudFront customers at no additional charge beyond standard AWS WAF pricing
- Content owners can monitor revenue and bot activity from a single dashboard in the AWS Management Console
