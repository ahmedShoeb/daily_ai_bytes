## Key Points

OpenRouter, an AI model API platform, has raised $75 million in Series B funding led by Thrive Capital
The funding round values the company at $1.3 billion - more than double its valuation from a year ago
OpenRouter operates like a "Kayak for AI models," allowing developers to discover and use hundreds of models
The platform offers flexible pricing including pay-per-use and subscription options
OpenRouter has experienced massive growth, processing 8.3 billion tokens in March 2026 compared to just 800 million tokens a year earlier
Founder and CEO Tushar Kant emphasizes that large model companies face commoditization risk, creating opportunities for companies like OpenRouter
Thrive Capital partner Kush Saxena will join OpenRouter's board of directors
This funding follows OpenRouter's $20 million Series A round the previous year
