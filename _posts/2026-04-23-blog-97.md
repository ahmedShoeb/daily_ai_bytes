## Key Points

AI agent adoption is rapidly escalating with 43% of organizations reporting that more than half of their employees now use AI agents daily
Shadow AI agents are appearing sooner than expected with 54% of organizations reporting 1-100 unsanctioned AI agents in their environment
Only 15% of organizations have defined ownership for most AI agents, leaving them dangerously unprepared during security incidents
Scope violations pose a constant threat - only 8% of organizations say their agents never exceed intended permissions
When incidents occur, 58% report that detection and response can take 5 hours or more, dangerously extending exposure across systems
Most organizations juggle four or more agentic platforms, intensifying the challenge of enforcing consistent security policies
Only 31% of organizations have formally adopted AI agent governance policies
Organizations are relying on compliance frameworks like HIPAA and NIST AI RMF that were built for static software, not autonomous systems operating at scale
