## Key Points

- OpenAI has released GPT-5.3 Instant, an update to ChatGPT's most-used model that improves everyday conversations
- The update focuses on making conversations more helpful and fluid by improving tone, relevance, and conversational flow
- GPT-5.3 Instant significantly reduces unnecessary refusals and overly cautious or preachy responses around sensitive topics
- The model provides more directly helpful answers without unnecessary caveats or dead ends
- Web search results are better contextualized, with improved balance between online information and the model's own knowledge
- Tone improvements cut back on 'cringe' moments, proclamations, and phrases like 'Stop. Take a breath'
- Hallucinations are reduced by 26.8% when using web search and 19.7% when relying only on internal knowledge in high-stakes domains
- The model is a stronger writing partner for fiction, creative prose, and expressive writing
- GPT-5.3 Instant is available now to all ChatGPT users and developers via API as 'gpt-5.3-chat-latest'
- GPT-5.2 Instant will remain available for paid users for three months before being retired on June 3, 2026
