## Key Points

- Cursor has launched a native iOS app in public beta, enabling developers to build from anywhere
- The mobile app allows launching always-on agents in the cloud or controlling agents running on your computer from your phone
- You can kick off agents when ideas strike, get notifications when work is ready for review, and merge PRs on the go
- The app supports voice input for describing ideas and slash commands to guide Cursor's direction
- Remote Control feature lets you direct agents running on your computer from your phone, with options to keep your computer awake while away
- Practical use cases include handling incidents while on call, resolving time-sensitive customer issues, and acting on feedback from other mobile apps
- Cursor provides updates via Live Activities on lock screens and push notifications for agent progress and completion
- Cloud agents run in isolated virtual machines with full development environments and can produce demos, screenshots, and logs
- You can handoff work between local and cloud environments, moving sessions back and forth as needed
- The iOS app is available now in public beta on all paid plans with special Composer 2.5 pricing through July 2026
