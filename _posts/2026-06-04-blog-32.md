## Key Points

NVIDIA Cosmos 3 is a new open world foundation model for physical AI built on a breakthrough mixture-of-transformers architecture
It's the world's first fully open omnimodel with native vision reasoning and multimodal generation across text, image, video, sound and actions
The model combines vision reasoning, world generation and action prediction in a single system
Cosmos 3 reduces physical AI training and evaluation cycles from months to days
NVIDIA launched the Cosmos Coalition with leading AI labs including Agile Robots, Black Forest Labs, Generalist, LTX, Runway and Skild AI
The architecture pairs a reasoning transformer with an expert generation transformer for understanding object interactions and spatial-temporal relationships
Trained on billions of multimodal samples across text, image, video, sound and action trajectories
Delivers leading results on physical AI benchmarks across Artificial Analysis, Physics-IQ, PAI-Bench and other leaderboards
Available in three versions: Cosmos 3 Super for highest accuracy, Cosmos 3 Nano for fast processing, and Cosmos 3 Edge (coming soon) for real-time inference
Developers can try Cosmos 3 on build.nvidia.com and download models from Hugging Face
