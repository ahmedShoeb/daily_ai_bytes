## Key Points

- Google announced Gemma 4 E2B, an on-device AI model designed specifically for Tensor G5 TPU
- The model was announced at I/O Connect India and will run natively on Pixel 10 series devices
- Gemma 4 is the base model for the upcoming Gemini Nano 4 model
- Features multimodal capabilities including offline AI chat, image recognition, and private audio transcription
- Supports Mobile Actions for controlling phone functions via voice or text commands
- Enables offline retail applications like converting recipes into in-store shopping maps
- Provides on-the-spot visual diagnostics for automotive mechanics
- Supports Pixel 10, 10 Pro, 10 Pro XL, and 10 Pro Fold devices
