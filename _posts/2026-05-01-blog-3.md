## Key Points

- Google is developing a new version of Jules as an end-to-end agentic product development platform
- The platform aims to help teams that are 'drowning in scattered context' like feedback, logs, and tickets
- Jules reads a team's entire product context to figure out what to build next
- The AI agent comes up with solutions and can ship a PR (pull request) directly
- This is an early experiment for teams with software products already in the market
- The waitlist is now open for product teams who want to help shape this new direction
- Google acknowledges they don't have all the answers yet and are looking for partners to co-develop the platform
