## Key Points

- Microsoft Azure offers a structured guide for application modernization called 'The App Modernization Playbook'
- The playbook helps organizations navigate the challenges of modernizing dozens or hundreds of applications
- Key focus on deciding which applications matter most and prioritizing modernization efforts
- Teaches how to assess application value, complexity, and opportunities for improvement
- Guides decisions on whether to modernize, replatform, refactor, or leave applications as-is based on business signals
- Helps match each application to the right Azure services and architecture without overengineering
- Provides methods to move from strategy to concrete execution with clear tradeoffs
- Features intelligent agents that automate discovery, assessments, and execution to accelerate modernization
- Led by Microsoft experts including Gayla Sheppard (Corporate VP Azure Data) and John Macintyre (Director of Product for Azure Analytics)
- Aims to transform overwhelming app landscapes into clear, actionable modernization plans
