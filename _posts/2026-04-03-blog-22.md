## Key Points

Google is introducing Veo 3.1 Lite, their most cost-effective video generation model for developers
The model costs less than 50% of Veo 3.1 Fast while maintaining the same speed
Veo 3.1 Lite supports both Text-to-Video and Image-to-Video generation
It offers flexible framing options including landscape (16:9) and portrait (9:16) ratios
The model supports 720p and 1080p video resolutions
Developers can customize video duration at 4s, 6s, or 8s with cost adjusting accordingly
Google will also reduce pricing for Veo 3.1 Fast on April 7th to make video generation more accessible
Veo 3.1 Lite is available starting today via paid tier on the Gemini API and Google AI Studio
The release completes the Veo 3.1 model family, giving developers flexibility based on their needs
This model is designed to empower developers building high-volume video applications
