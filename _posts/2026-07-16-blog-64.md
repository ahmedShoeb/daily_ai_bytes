## Key Points

Anthropic is scheduling meetings with investors ahead of a potential IPO later this year
Bankers are arranging meetings between prospective investors and Anthropic executives
The AI startup confidentially filed its IPO prospectus with the SEC last month
Anthropic could go public as soon as October, though timing could change
The company closed a $65 billion funding round at a $965 billion valuation in May
This would make Anthropic the first major AI company to hit public markets
Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, and JPMorgan Chase are leading the IPO planning
Anthropic appears poised to beat rival OpenAI to the public markets
