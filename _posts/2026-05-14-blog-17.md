## Key Points

- The article discusses the ongoing shift in AI from model training to inference as the primary cost driver and business challenge
- Hardware companies like Nvidia are profiting from the training phase, but inference creates different economic dynamics
- AI models will become cheaper and more accessible over time due to improvements in algorithms and hardware efficiency
- This creates pressure on AI companies to find sustainable business models as the technology becomes commoditized
- Inference costs are becoming the critical factor in AI deployment and profitability
