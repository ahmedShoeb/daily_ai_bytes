## Key Points

Mistral AI has launched OCR 4, featuring new capabilities like bounding boxes, block classification, and inline confidence scores alongside extracted text
The model supports 170 languages across 10 language groups and achieves breakthrough performance with 72% win rates against leading OCR systems in human evaluations
OCR 4 achieves top scores on public benchmarks (85.20 on OlmOCRBench) and leads internal multilingual evaluations across all language groups
The model can be deployed in a single container for fully self-hosted deployments, keeping document data within enterprise infrastructure for compliance
OCR 4 is priced at $4 per 1,000 pages via API, with a 50% Batch-API discount bringing it to $2 per 1,000 pages
The model provides structured output including bounding boxes for text localization, block classification (titles, tables, equations, signatures), and confidence scores
OCR 4 integrates with Mistral Search Toolkit (public preview) as an ingestion component for RAG and enterprise search applications
Early users are applying OCR 4 to turn invoices into structured fields, digitize company archives, and extract clean text from technical reports
The model is available through Mistral Studio, Amazon SageMaker, Microsoft Foundry, and will soon be available on Snowflake Parse Document
OCR 4 maintains high accuracy on specialized and low-resource languages where competing systems typically degrade sharply
