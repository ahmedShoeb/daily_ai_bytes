## Key Points

Anthropic is releasing ten ready-to-run agent templates for financial services, including pitch builders, meeting preparers, earnings reviewers, and KYC screeners
These agents can be deployed as plugins in Claude Cowork and Claude Code, or as cookbooks for Claude Managed Agents, allowing teams to implement AI solutions in days rather than months
Claude now works across Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, Word, and Outlook (coming soon) with add-ins that carry context automatically between applications
The system is expanding its partner ecosystem with new connectors from companies like Dun & Bradstreet, FactSet, PitchBook, and Moody's, providing governed access to financial data
Claude Opus 4.7 leads the industry on financial tasks, scoring 64.37% on Vals AI's Finance Agent benchmark
The agent templates package skills, connectors, and subagents that can be adapted to firms' own modeling conventions and risk policies
Financial services use cases cover front office (research, client experience), middle office (underwriting, risk, compliance), and back office (operations) work
Users maintain oversight throughout the process, reviewing and approving Claude's work before it's sent to clients or filed
Major financial institutions like FIS, Carlyle, and Walleye Capital are already using Claude for various financial workflows
The Claude for Excel add-in allows investment professionals to build and update financial models directly within Excel
