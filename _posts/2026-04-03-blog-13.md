## Key Points

- OpenAI closed its latest funding round with $122 billion in committed capital at a post-money valuation of $852 billion
- The round was anchored by strategic partners Amazon, NVIDIA, and SoftBank, with continued participation from Microsoft
- OpenAI is now generating $2 billion in revenue per month, with enterprise revenue making up over 40% of total revenue
- ChatGPT has over 900 million weekly active users and more than 50 million subscribers, with 6x the monthly web visits compared to the next largest AI app
- OpenAI recently launched GPT-5.4, its most capable model yet, and expanded Codex into a flagship coding agent
- The company's APIs now process more than 15 billion tokens per minute, and Codex serves over 2 million weekly users
- OpenAI is building a broader infrastructure portfolio across multiple cloud partners, chip platforms, and data centers
- The company plans to build a unified AI superapp that brings together ChatGPT, Codex, browsing, and agentic capabilities into one agent-first experience
- OpenAI is becoming the core infrastructure for AI, with a flywheel effect where more compute drives more intelligent models, which drive better products and faster adoption
- The funding round included participation from global institutions and for the first time extended to individual investors through bank channels, raising over $3 billion
