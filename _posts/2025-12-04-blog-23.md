## Key Points

German AI lab Black Forest Labs secured $300 million in Series B funding.
The funding round values the company at $3.25 billion.
Salesforce Ventures and Anjney Midha (AMP) co-led the investment, with participation from NVIDIA, a16z, and others.
The company specializes in foundation AI models for generating and editing images.
Black Forest Labs' models are utilized by major platforms including Adobe, Elon Musk’s Grok chatbot, and ElevenLabs.
They recently launched Flux 2, their latest image-generation model, which offers enhanced rendering and multi-image referencing.
The co-founders previously contributed to the development of Stability AI’s Stable Diffusion models.

## Key Topics Discussed

Hey everyone, let's dive into some exciting news from the AI world! German AI powerhouse Black Forest Labs just announced a massive win, raising a whopping $300 million in Series B funding. This latest round catapults their valuation to an impressive $3.25 billion! The investment, co-led by Salesforce Ventures and Anjney Midha, also saw big names like a16z, NVIDIA, and Temasek jumping in to support their vision. Black Forest Labs, which has quickly gained prominence since launching in August 2024, is all about creating cutting-edge foundation AI models for generating and editing images. You might have even seen their work in action, as their models are powering image generation for a host of companies, including Adobe, fal.ai, Picsart, and even Elon Musk's Grok chatbot! They’re not just stopping there; the company recently unveiled Flux 2, their newest image-generation model, which boasts better text and image rendering and can use up to ten reference images to nail down the perfect style and tone. It can even generate images up to 4K resolution! And get this, the brilliant minds behind Black Forest Labs – Robin Rombach, Patrick Esser, and Andreas Blattmann – were previously instrumental in developing Stability AI’s renowned Stable Diffusion models. This funding will fuel their ongoing research and development, so get ready to see even more innovation from them!
