## Key Points

- Sakana AI released Fugu and Fugu Ultra as commercial APIs for multi-model orchestration, turning research into a product
- Fugu Ultra scored 93.2 on LiveCodeBench, outperforming Claude Fable 5's 89.8 and matching or beating top models on other benchmarks
- The launch follows Anthropic's suspension of Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 access due to US government directives
- Fugu operates as a single API endpoint that routes work across multiple models, reducing dependence on any single provider
- Pricing starts at $5 per million input tokens and $30 per million output tokens for Fugu Ultra
- Critics note Fugu adds a 'black box' layer since users can't see which specific models handle each task
- The service is not available in EU/EEA regions while Sakana addresses data compliance issues
- Nearly 500 beta users tested Fugu before launch in areas like code review, security assessment, and automated research
- Sakana plans to add open models and its own models to the routing pool over time
- The company pitches Fugu as a solution to vendor lock-in and dependence on single providers for critical infrastructure
