## Key Points

1. Cognition has acquired TierZero and welcomes Anhang, Yun, and their team to the company.
2. The blog post emphasizes that great software engineering involves more than just writing code - it includes maintenance, incident prevention, and system health monitoring.
3. TierZero's expertise focuses on automation that keeps software running smoothly after deployment.
4. Cognition plans to integrate TierZero's automation work into their Devin platform.
5. The goal is to reduce the time engineers spend handling incidents and increase the time they spend building new features.
6. The acquisition represents a strategic move to enhance Cognition's capabilities in software maintenance and operations automation.
7. The announcement was made by Walden Yan on July 20, 2026.
