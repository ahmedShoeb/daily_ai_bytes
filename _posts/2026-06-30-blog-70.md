## Key Points

- fal.ai has released AuraSR, a 600M parameter upsampler model derived from the GigaGAN paper
- AuraSR can upscale low-resolution images to 4x the resolution and can be applied repeatedly
- The model is published under a truly open source license with no limitations on resolution or upscaling factor
- AuraSR excels at upscaling images generated by text-to-image models
- GAN-based upscalers like AuraSR are orders of magnitude faster than diffusion models, generating 1024px images in just 0.25 seconds
- The model is based on lucidrains' GitHub implementation of GigaGAN
- The company is currently hiring for training open source models
