## Key Points

Google has introduced Gemini Embedding 2, a fully multimodal embedding model now available in Public Preview through the Gemini API and Vertex AI
The model creates a unified embedding space for text, images, videos, audio, and documents, supporting over 100 languages
Developers, AI researchers, and enterprise teams can now access the model globally wherever Gemini API and Vertex AI are offered
Gemini Embedding 2 supports multiple modalities in a single request, processing up to 8192 text tokens, six images (PNG, JPEG), videos up to 120 seconds, native audio, and PDFs up to six pages
The model features Matryoshka Representation Learning, allowing users to choose between 3072, 1536, or 768 output dimensions to optimize for storage and performance
Early access partners have already started using the model for advanced AI tasks like Retrieval-Augmented Generation, semantic search, and data clustering
Google's Gemini architecture forms the backbone of this release, building on the company's extensive expertise in large language models and multimodal understanding
Early industry feedback indicates Gemini Embedding 2 outperforms leading competitors in text, image, and video benchmarks, setting a new standard for multimodal AI
