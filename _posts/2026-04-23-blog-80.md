## Key Points

BAR (Branch-Adapt-Route) introduces modular post-training with mixture-of-experts
Rather than training a single model on all data at once, BAR trains independent domain experts through complete training pipelines
BAR's three-stage approach: 1) Independent expert training, 2) Expert merging, 3) Router training
BAR outperforms baselines that don't require rerunning mid-training from scratch
BAR allows experts to be upgraded independently without affecting other domains
Modular training avoids degradation of safety capabilities learned during earlier stages
BAR provides linear cost scaling for domain updates compared to quadratic cost of monolithic retraining
BAR's approach aligns with practical large-scale model development where different teams work on different capabilities
