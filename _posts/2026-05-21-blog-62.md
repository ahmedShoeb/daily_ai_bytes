## Key Points

- Qwen3.5-397B-A17B is the first open-weight model in the Qwen3.5 series, featuring native multimodal capabilities
- The model is trained for real-world agents and powered by hybrid linear attention + sparse MoE architecture
- Offers 8.6x–19.0x decoding throughput compared to Qwen3-Max
- Supports 201 languages and dialects
- Apache 2.0 licensed
- Qwen-Image-2512 update brings more realistic humans with reduced 'AI look' and richer facial details
- Qwen3-Omni is the first natively end-to-end omni-modal AI unifying text, image, audio & video in one model
- Achieves SOTA on 22/36 audio & AV benchmarks
- Features 119 language text support, 19 language speech input, and 10 language speech output
- Includes Qwen-Image-Edit with precise bilingual text editing capabilities for Chinese and English
