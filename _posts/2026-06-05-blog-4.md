## Key Points

- Microsoft AI announced seven new in-house models forming the MAI model family across multiple domains
- MAI-Thinking-1 is Microsoft's flagship reasoning model that matches leading models on software engineering benchmarks and outperforms Sonnet 4.6 in blind human evaluations
- MAI-Code-1-Flash is an efficient coding model integrated into GitHub Copilot and VS Code, comparable to Haiku but cheaper
- MAI-Image-2.5 supports text-to-image and image editing, surpassing Nano Banana Pro in Arena scores
- MAI Transcribe-1.5 claims to be the world's best transcription model with 5x speed improvement and support for 43 languages
- Microsoft introduced Microsoft Frontier Tuning allowing models to adapt to specific organizational workflows using reinforcement learning
- Microsoft and Mayo Clinic announced collaboration to co-create a frontier AI model for healthcare applications
- The article emphasizes Microsoft's commitment to training models from scratch with clean, licensed data rather than distilling from other labs
- Microsoft describes building a 'hill-climbing machine' organization focused on continuous improvement through compute, data, and evaluation
- Models will be available on multiple platforms including Foundry, OpenRouter, Fireworks, and Baseten
