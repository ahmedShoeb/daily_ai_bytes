## Key Points

OpenAI Daybreak is a new vision to change how software is built and defended by integrating AI into the development process
The platform aims to make software 'resilient by design' rather than just finding and patching vulnerabilities
AI helps defenders scan codebases, identify vulnerabilities, validate fixes, analyze systems, and accelerate remediation
The system pairs expanded defensive capabilities with trust, verification, proportional safeguards, and accountability
Daybreak combines OpenAI models with Codex as an agentic harness and security partners to accelerate cyber defenders
Key features include secure code review, threat modeling, patch validation, dependency risk analysis, detection, and remediation guidance
OpenAI is offering three tiers: GPT-5.5 (default), GPT-5.5 with Trusted Access for Cyber, and GPT-5.5-Cyber for specialized authorized workflows
The platform targets high-impact threats, reduces analysis time from hours to minutes, and enables safe patching at scale
Verification features send audit-ready evidence back to systems to track and verify remediation
OpenAI is working with industry and government partners for iterative deployment in coming weeks
