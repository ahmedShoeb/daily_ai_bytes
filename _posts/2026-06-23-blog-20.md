## Key Points

e
- Anthropic has completely shut off access to its newly launched Mythos 5 and Fable 5 AI models
- The shutdown came after Anthropic received a US Commerce Department directive on Friday evening
- The directive subjects the new models to export controls restricting their use outside the United States
- Anthropic stated the only way to ensure immediate compliance was to disable the models for all customers
- The Trump administration reportedly requested a pause due to concerns about a jailbreak that bypasses safeguards for cybersecurity, chemistry, and biology
- The administration wants time for the 'national security apparatus' to be 'hardened' against this threat, possibly within weeks
- Anthropic claims the government only provided 'verbal evidence of a potential narrow, non-universal jailbreak'
- The company says the jailbreak reportedly helps find 'minor' and 'relatively simple' software vulnerabilities
- Anthropic notes that other publicly available models like GPT-5.5 have similar capabilities
- Access to other Anthropic models remains unaffected
