## Key Points

- ElevenLabs launched its first iOS app called "ElevenLabs Reader: AI Audio" focused on converting text and web links into audio narration
- The app is available for free download from the Apple App Store and requires iOS 15 or newer
- Users can listen to audio narration while on the go or multitasking with pre-loaded public domain stories like Cinderella and Sherlock Holmes
- The app allows users to add their own content by writing text, importing URLs from browsers, or uploading files from iOS Files app
- Users can choose from 11 different voices and accents (American, Austrian, British English) and adjust playback speed from 0.8x to 2x
- The app integrates with iOS Share Sheet, allowing users to share links directly from Safari browser
- Initial testing showed some formatting issues with PDFs and occasional error messages, but overall functionality was fluid and accurate
- The text-to-speech functionality is currently only available in English, with plans to add support for 29+ languages soon
- The app is more narrowly focused compared to ElevenLabs' full website which includes text-to-speech, voice conversion, AI dubbing, and sound effects
