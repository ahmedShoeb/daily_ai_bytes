## Key Points

Meta has launched Facebook AI Mode, a new search experience that uses Meta AI to provide conversational answers synthesized from public posts
The tool mines years of user-generated content across Groups, Reels, and Marketplace listings to answer queries in plain language
Currently rolling out to US users, the feature seems inspired by Google Search's AI Mode but focuses on social interactions
AI Mode can pull practical advice from local Group discussions, recommend Marketplace product listings, or surface helpful Reels clips
This follows the launch of Forum, a standalone Reddit-style app for Facebook Groups with its own AI 'Ask' tab
Serious concerns exist about reliability since AI summarizes user comments rather than verified databases, risking outdated or unverified information
Meta hasn't disclosed if users can opt their public text out of the data pool or how private content is handled
The new search is part of Meta's broader software expansion including animated profile pictures and AI text replies for Marketplace
Meta also introduced Plus membership packages starting at $3.99/month for Facebook and Instagram with advanced AI tiers planned
