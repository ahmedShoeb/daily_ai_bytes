## Key Points

- IndexCache is a patch for SGLang and vLLM that accelerates inference for models using DeepSeek Sparse Attention (DSA), including DeepSeek-V3.2 and GLM-5
- It eliminates up to 75% of indexer computations through cross-layer index reuse, with up to 1.82× prefill speedup and 1.48× decode speedup
- At 200K context length, the indexer consumes 81% of prefill time in baseline DSA, making it the dominant bottleneck
- The approach discovers that adjacent layers share 70-100% of their selected tokens, meaning most indexer computations are redundant
- IndexCache partitions layers into Full layers (keep their indexer) and Shared layers (reuse cached indices from nearest Full layer)
- Two approaches: training-free (greedy search to remove indexers) and training-aware (multi-layer distillation)
- Benchmarks show virtually unchanged results across 9 tests with ~1.2× end-to-end speedup and negligible quality degradation
- Configuration options include index_topk_freq (keep indexer every N layers) and index_topk_pattern (per-layer F/S assignment)
- The patch is compatible with any model using DSA indexer architecture
