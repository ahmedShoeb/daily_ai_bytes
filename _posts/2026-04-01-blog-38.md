## Key Points

- Analysis of billions of anonymized credit card transactions shows Claude gaining paid subscribers in record numbers, especially between January and February 2026
- Anthropic confirmed that Claude paid subscriptions have more than doubled this year
- The majority of new subscribers are at the $20 per month "Pro" tier (compared to $100 or $200 per month tiers)
- Multiple factors contributed to increased awareness: Super Bowl commercials mocking ChatGPT's ads, the DOD feud, and new product releases
- Anthropic released Super Bowl commercials that mocked ChatGPT's decision to show ads, promising Claude would never do the same
- A public feud with the Department of Defense (DOD) in late January brought attention when Anthropic refused to allow its AI for lethal autonomous operations or mass surveillance of citizens
- New features like Claude Code, Claude Cowork (released in January), and Computer Use (released this week) have driven subscriptions
- Computer Use allows Claude to navigate computers independently (clicking, scrolling, taking actions), and works with Dispatch for phone-based task assignment
- Despite rapid growth, Claude remains far behind ChatGPT in the consumer AI market, though OpenAI experienced uninstalls after announcing a DOD deal
