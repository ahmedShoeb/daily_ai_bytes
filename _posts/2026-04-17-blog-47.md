## Key Points

- Google DeepMind introduces Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6, an upgraded reasoning-first model that enables robots to understand physical environments with unprecedented precision
- The model enhances spatial reasoning, multi-view understanding, and task planning capabilities for autonomous robotics
- Key new capability is instrument reading, allowing robots to read complex gauges and sight glasses developed through collaboration with Boston Dynamics
- Features advanced pointing capabilities for spatial reasoning, relational logic, motion reasoning, and constraint compliance
- Includes sophisticated success detection that enables robots to know when tasks are complete and make intelligent decisions about retrying or moving forward
- Uses agentic vision combining visual reasoning with code execution to achieve accurate instrument readings in industrial settings
- Demonstrates superior safety compliance with Gemini safety policies, making safer decisions about physical manipulation and identifying safety hazards
- Outperforms both Gemini Robotics-ER 1.5 and Gemini 3.0 Flash in spatial and physical reasoning tasks
- Available now to developers via Gemini API and Google AI Studio with accompanying Colab examples
- Partnership with Boston Dynamics focuses on facility inspection use cases where robots like Spot can autonomously monitor instruments in industrial facilities
