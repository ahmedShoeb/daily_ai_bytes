## Key Points

- Codex now has over 5 million weekly users, with non-developers making up 20% of users and growing 3x faster than developers
- OpenAI is introducing role-specific plugins that adapt Codex to different professions and tools without coding required
- Six new plugins launched: data analytics, creative production, sales, product design, public equity investing, and investment banking
- Each plugin bundles relevant apps, skills, and workflows (62 apps and 110 skills total across all plugins)
- Codex can now create and share interactive websites and apps ('Sites') that can be shared via URL within workspaces
- Annotations feature allows users to refine specific parts of content (like documents, spreadsheets, and slides) without starting over
- More role-specific plugins coming soon including Corporate Finance, Private Equity Investing, Marketing Strategy, Strategy Consulting, and Legal
- Sites feature is in preview for Business and Enterprise customers, with partnerships including Vercel, Wix, Replit, Figma, etc.
- Teams at Zapier and NVIDIA already use Codex for various workflows like creating incident response plans and speeding up ML research
- Plugins work out of the box but teams can also customize them or build their own plugins
