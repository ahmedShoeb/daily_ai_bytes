## Key Points

- Sakana AI has launched its first commercial product called Sakana Marlin, an autonomous research assistant for business
- Sakana Marlin can work autonomously for up to 8 hours on research tasks, generating structured summary slides and detailed reports up to 100 pages
- The product is designed to handle substantial strategy research similar to what a Chief Strategy Officer (CSO) and team might spend weeks on
- Users simply input a research topic, and Marlin autonomously refines the direction, forms hypotheses, gathers information, and verifies findings
- Marlin goes beyond summarization to map causal relationships in complex business environments and organize them into structured strategic options
- The technology builds on Sakana AI's research in long-horizon reasoning, including their Nature-published AI Scientist work and AB-MCTS techniques
- The product was refined through a closed beta with around 300 professionals from financial institutions, consulting firms, and other industries
- Sakana Marlin is available through a pay-per-use model with Pro, Team, and Enterprise tier plans
- The launch represents an important step in Sakana AI's commercial rollout beyond their chat services
- The company continues to focus on systems where multiple models work together rather than relying on single models
