## Key Points

Hy3 is a 295B-parameter Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) model from Tencent's Hy Team in China
It has 21B active parameters and 3.8B MTP layer parameters, released under Apache 2.0 license
The model outperforms similar-size models and rivals flagship open-source models with 2-5x its parameters
Available in two sizes: full-sized model (598GB) and FP8 quantized version (300GB)
Context length is 256K tokens
Free on OpenRouter until July 21st, 2026
Model was previously previewed in late April 2026 and tested across 50+ products
Significant gains reported in utility across various products and productivity tasks
Author tested the model by generating an SVG of a pelican riding a bicycle
