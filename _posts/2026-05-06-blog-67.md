## Key Points

Anthropic is conducting red team testing on a new internal build called Claude Jupiter V1 - this follows their pattern of using planet names for pre-release safety probes rather than product labels
The timing suggests a potential announcement at Anthropic's Code with Claude developer conference in San Francisco on May 6th, with London and Tokyo dates following later in spring
This pattern matches last year's approach when the company tested under the codename Neptune just weeks before unveiling the Claude 4 family in mid-May 2025
The current lineup has Opus 4.7 as flagship, but Sonnet 4.7 and Haiku 4.7 are conspicuously absent - potentially leaving room for either a refresh or new generation models
Jupiter being the largest planet in the solar system has led to speculation about the potential scale or importance of this release
If released on schedule, the new model would appear across Anthropic Platform, Claude Code, and Claude consumer apps, similar to how Opus 4.7 rolled out earlier this spring
The red team testing aligns with Anthropic's responsible scaling policy requiring jailbreak probes and constitutional classifier stress tests before frontier-class deployments
