## Key Points

Mistral 3 is the new generation of open multimodal and multilingual AI models, including dense models (3B, 8B, 14B) and a sparse mixture-of-experts model, Mistral Large 3 (675B total parameters).
All Mistral 3 models are released under the Apache 2.0 license, promoting open-sourcing and distributed intelligence.
Mistral Large 3 is a state-of-the-art open model, trained on 3000 NVIDIA H200 GPUs, offering top performance in general prompts, image understanding, and best-in-class multilingual conversations.
The Ministral 3 series provides state-of-the-art intelligence for edge and local use cases, with base, instruct, and reasoning variants, all with image understanding.
Mistral, NVIDIA, vLLM, and Red Hat partnered to deliver faster and more accessible Mistral 3 through optimization and checkpoint releases.
Mistral 3 is immediately available on multiple platforms, including Mistral AI Studio, Amazon Bedrock, and Hugging Face, with more coming soon.
Mistral AI offers custom model training services for tailored enterprise AI solutions.
Mistral 3 emphasizes frontier performance with open access, multimodal and multilingual capabilities across 40+ languages, scalable efficiency, and agentic adaptability for diverse applications.

## Key Topics Discussed

Hey everyone, get ready to hear about something big in the AI world! Mistral AI has just announced Mistral 3, their next-generation family of open, multimodal, and multilingual AI models. This release brings a whole new level of innovation with both small, dense models and the incredibly capable Mistral Large 3. We're talking about models ranging from 3 billion to a whopping 675 billion total parameters, and here's the best part: they're all open-sourced under the Apache 2.0 license, putting powerful AI directly into the hands of developers.

Mistral Large 3 is a true powerhouse, trained on thousands of NVIDIA H200 GPUs. It's their first mixture-of-experts model since the Mixtral series, and it's making waves by achieving performance parity with some of the best instruction-tuned open-weight models out there. Not only does it excel in general prompts, but it also boasts impressive image understanding and best-in-class performance in multilingual conversations, going beyond just English and Chinese. It even debuted at #2 on the LMArena leaderboard for OSS non-reasoning models!

But it's not just about the large models. For those looking for AI at the edge or for local applications, Mistral is introducing the Ministral 3 series. These models come in 3B, 8B, and 14B sizes, each with base, instruct, and reasoning variants, all equipped with image understanding capabilities. They're designed to offer the best cost-to-performance ratio in the open-source market, delivering high accuracy while being incredibly efficient.

Mistral AI has also teamed up with NVIDIA, vLLM, and Red Hat to ensure these advanced models are super accessible. They're releasing optimized checkpoints that allow for efficient deployment on various systems, from large data centers to RTX PCs and even Jetson devices. This collaborative effort truly highlights their commitment to making cutting-edge AI widely available.

The availability of Mistral 3 is impressive, launching today across platforms like Mistral AI Studio, Amazon Bedrock, Azure Foundry, Hugging Face, and more, with plans for NVIDIA NIM and AWS SageMaker coming soon. And for organizations with specific needs, Mistral AI is offering custom model training services to fine-tune or adapt these models for enterprise-grade deployments, ensuring maximum impact and security.

The future of AI is definitely looking open and exciting with Mistral 3. It's redefining what's possible with frontier performance, multimodal and multilingual flexibility across 40+ languages, scalable efficiency, and adaptability for a wide range of tasks, from coding to creative collaboration and document analysis. This release empowers developers and enterprises alike to explore, build, and innovate, turning understanding into action. So, if you're into AI, this is a release you'll definitely want to check out!
