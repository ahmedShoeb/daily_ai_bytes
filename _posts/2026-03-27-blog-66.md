## Key Points

- Databricks announced Lakewatch, a new open, agentic SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) solution to help organizations defend against AI-driven attacks
- Lakewatch unifies security, IT, and business data into a single, governed environment for AI detection and response
- Key capabilities include agentic triage and investigation, automated security intelligence with Genie, open ecosystem integration, detection-as-code, and governance/compliance at scale
- The solution addresses the asymmetry where attackers use AI agents to attack anywhere while defenders face incomplete data and manual workflows
- Lakewatch enables unlimited data ingestion and petabyte-scale threat detection at up to 80% lower total cost of ownership (TCO)
- Databricks is deepening its partnership with Anthropic to power Lakewatch with Claude models for advanced threat correlation
- The company also announced acquisitions of Antimatter (AI agent security) and SiftD.ai (detection engineering expertise)
- Lakewatch is now available in Private Preview and is already used by enterprise customers including Adobe and Dropbox
