## Key Points

– Thinking Machines has released Inkling, their first model trained from scratch with full weights available for customization
- Inkling is a Mixture-of-Experts transformer with 975B total parameters (41B active) and supports a 1M token context window
- The model was pretrained on 45 trillion tokens of text, images, audio, and video data
- Key features include natively reasoning over text, images, and audio, plus controllable thinking effort for balancing performance with cost
- Inkling shows strong performance across agentic coding, reasoning, factuality, vision, and audio benchmarks
- The company is also previewing Inkling-Small, a lighter model with 12B active parameters for lower cost/latency workloads
- Inkling is available for fine-tuning on their Tinker platform today with a 50% introductory discount
- The model demonstrates strong safety features and built-in safeguards against harmful requests
- External partnerships make Inkling available through APIs on platforms like TogetherAI, Fireworks, Modal, Databricks, and Baseten
