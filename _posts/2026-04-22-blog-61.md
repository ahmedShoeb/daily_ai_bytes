## Key Points

DeepSeek-V3 is a massive 671-billion parameter mixture-of-experts (MoE) model
The model uses 128 experts with 37 billion active parameters during inference
It achieves comparable performance to leading models like Llama-3.1-405B, Claude-3.5-Sonnet, and GPT-4o across multiple benchmarks
The architecture implements a mixture-of-experts design that keeps most parameters inactive during inference to reduce computational costs
Training data includes 6.9 trillion tokens from diverse sources, with a context window of 128K tokens
DeepSeek-V3 demonstrates strong capabilities in math, coding, reasoning, and knowledge tasks
The model shows efficient scaling properties and strong performance-to-cost ratio
It's trained on high-quality multilingual data, with 30% non-English content
