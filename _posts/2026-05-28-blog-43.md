## Key Points

Apple is expected to preview iOS 27 at WWDC in June 2026 with a major focus on advancing Apple Intelligence capabilities
Bloomberg's Mark Gurman reports significant visual quality improvements coming to Genmoji and Image Playground AI image tools
Apple's in-house AI models powering these features have been significantly improved for better output quality
Genmoji may become more proactive by suggesting emojis based on users' photo libraries and frequently used phrases, moving beyond manual text prompts
Apple may integrate additional third-party AI models beyond ChatGPT, potentially including Google's AI systems
The update will include an AI-powered wallpaper generator using Image Playground technology
iOS 27 is also rumored to bring a redesigned Siri, overhauled Shortcuts app with AI-based shortcut creation, and improved Writing Tools
The improvements aim to help Apple better compete with Android and Google Pixel's existing AI features
Apple originally introduced Genmoji and Image Playground in iOS 18, with both tools enabling AI-generated content creation
The company is expanding its creative ecosystem by potentially allowing more third-party AI image-generation models to integrate
