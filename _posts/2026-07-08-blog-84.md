## Key Points

ByteDance appears close to releasing Dreamina Seedance 2.5, with rumors pointing to a possible July 9 launch date
The main new feature will be a move from short clips to 30-second standard video generation with the ability to extend up to 180 seconds
Dreamina pages describe a long video workflow that can generate 30-second scenes, 90-second drafts, and 180-second (3-minute) outputs
Availability is expected across Dreamina, CapCut, and partner platforms that already work with ByteDance's video stack
Seedance 2.5 would transform the tool from a short-shot generator into a solution for longer sequences suitable for creators, advertisers, and AI filmmakers
The model aims to maintain character identity, motion, camera logic, and prompt intent stable across video extensions
ByteDance has been integrating AI video into its creator ecosystem through Dreamina, CapCut, TikTok-adjacent production flows, and API distribution
Seedance 2.5 pushes ByteDance's strategy toward longer commercial storytelling and creator workflows
This release comes as competitive pressure mounts with Google's Veo and Gemini Omni, following OpenAI's discontinuation of Sora's web and app product
