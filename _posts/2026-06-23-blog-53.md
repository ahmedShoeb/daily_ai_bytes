## Key Points

- iOS 27 introduces a major overhaul to AirPods settings in the Settings app
- The new design organizes AirPods settings into proper menus with accompanying icons for easier identification
- AirPods settings still appear at the top of the Settings app but only when AirPods are actively connected
- The update condenses previously lengthy and disorganized toggles into a more user-friendly interface
- This addresses the problem of AirPods settings becoming hard to navigate due to numerous features added over the years
- The article expresses hope for a dedicated AirPods app eventually but calls this a 'great improvement' over iOS 26
- The redesign makes the main AirPods settings screen much shorter and easier to use
- iOS 27 was introduced at WWDC with the first developer beta now available
