## Key Points

- The project is building a public option for AI that is open, auditable, and serves people over profit
- They've mapped the open source AI stack to identify gaps in the current ecosystem
- Evaluated over 24,626 projects from foundation models through inference backends
- Assessed projects across three criteria: openness, capability, and adoption
- Collaborated with open source AI experts from Columbia Convening, MOF, and Hugging Face
- The initiative is seeking collaborators to review products, improve methodology, or add tools
