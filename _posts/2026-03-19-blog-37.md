## Key Points

- Mistral Small 4 is a new major release that combines reasoning, multimodal, and coding capabilities into a single model
- It's the first Mistral model to unify Magistral (reasoning), Pixtral (multimodal), and Devstral (coding) capabilities
- Released under Apache 2.0 license, continuing Mistral's commitment to open-source AI
- Features Mixture of Experts (MoE) architecture with 128 experts, 4 active per token, and 119B total parameters
- Has a 256k context window and supports both text and image inputs natively
- Offers configurable reasoning effort with toggle between fast responses and deep reasoning
- Delivers 40% reduction in end-to-end completion time and 3x more requests per second compared to Mistral Small 3
- Requires minimum infrastructure of 4x NVIDIA HGX H100, 2x NVIDIA HGX H200, or 1x NVIDIA DGX B200
- Available on Mistral API, AI Studio, NVIDIA NIM, and various open-source platforms like vLLM and llama.cpp
- Achieves competitive benchmark scores while generating significantly shorter outputs compared to competitors
