## Key Points

Meta introduces LLM Compiler, a suite of pre-trained models specifically designed for code optimization tasks
Built on Code Llama foundation, LLM Compiler enhances understanding of compiler intermediate representations, assembly language, and optimization techniques
Trained on 546 billion tokens of LLVM-IR and assembly code, making it one of the largest datasets for compiler optimization models
Available in two sizes: 7 billion and 13 billion parameters
Released under a bespoke commercial license to allow wide reuse by both academic researchers and industry practitioners
Achieves 77% of the optimization potential of an autotuning search for code size optimization
Capable of disassembling x86_64 and ARM assembly back into LLVM-IR with 45% round trip success (14% exact match)
Addresses the resource-intensive challenge of training LLMs for compiler optimization by providing pre-trained foundation models
Aims to make compiler optimization more scalable and cost-effective for further research and development
