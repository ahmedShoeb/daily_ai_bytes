## Key Points

OpenAI is raising an additional $10 billion from investors, bringing its total record fundraise to over $120 billion
This exceeds the company's initial target of $100 billion and may be its last private fundraise before a potential IPO
Notable participants include Microsoft, Andreessen Horowitz, D.E. Shaw Ventures, MGX, TPG, and T. Rowe Price
The fresh investment comes about a month after OpenAI announced a $110 billion raise at a $730 billion pre-money valuation
Amazon previously invested $50 billion, while Nvidia and SoftBank each committed $30 billion in the earlier wave
OpenAI and Amazon have a multi-year partnership to build custom models for Amazon's customer-facing applications
OpenAI reported $13.1 billion in revenue last year, with 60% from consumers and 40% from enterprise accounts
The company is balancing growth with moderation, now targeting $600 billion in compute spend through 2030 instead of earlier $1.4 trillion commitments
OpenAI recently shut down its short-form video app Sora to prioritize more profitable initiatives
CFO Sarah Friar stated the company is 
building toward
public company readiness, though timing depends on market conditions
