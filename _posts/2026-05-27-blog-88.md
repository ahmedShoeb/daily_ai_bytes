## Key Points

60% of enterprises are investing millions in agentic AI, but only 15% have the data foundation to run it securely and effectively at scale
Organizations are moving aggressively into agentic AI without the underlying infrastructure to support it at scale
Weak data foundations are limiting AI outcomes, with gaps in consistency, governance, and control slowing progress from pilots to production
Interoperability is critical as AI depends on seamless data access across tools, but most systems remain fragmented and tightly coupled
The report is based on a global survey of 400 data leaders and explores what separates successful organizations from those stuck in costly experiments
