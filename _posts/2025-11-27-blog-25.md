## Key Points

OpenAI is introducing a new shopping research experience in ChatGPT.
This feature helps users find the right products by doing the research for them.
It asks clarifying questions, researches across the internet, reviews quality sources, and uses past conversations and memory for personalized buyer's guides.
Shopping research is rolling out on mobile and web for logged-in ChatGPT users on Free, Go, Plus, and Pro plans, with nearly unlimited usage through the holidays.
It performs well in detail-heavy categories like electronics, beauty, home and garden, kitchen and appliances, and sports and outdoor.
The feature can be accessed by asking a shopping question or selecting 'shopping research' from the (+) menu.
It provides a visual interface for interaction, allowing users to guide the research by sharing feedback.
It gathers up-to-date information like price, availability, reviews, specs, and images, and presents a personalized buyer's guide.
Shopping research is powered by a version of GPT-5 mini trained with reinforcement learning specifically for shopping tasks.
It is designed to be transparent, not sharing chats with retailers, and providing organic results based on publicly available retail sites.
Users are encouraged to visit merchant sites for the most accurate details, as the model might still make mistakes.

## Key Topics Discussed

Hey everyone, let's talk about something super exciting from OpenAI! They've just rolled out a brand-new feature in ChatGPT called "shopping research," and it's designed to make finding the perfect product so much easier. Instead of spending hours sifting through countless websites, you can now just tell ChatGPT what you're looking for, and it will do the heavy lifting for you. It's pretty smart too, asking you clarifying questions and using your past conversations and memory to create a truly personalized buyer's guide. 

This new tool is available right now on both mobile and web for all logged-in ChatGPT users, no matter if you're on a Free, Go, Plus, or Pro plan. And get this – they're offering nearly unlimited usage through the holidays, which is perfect for all that gift shopping! It's especially good for those tricky, detail-heavy purchases in categories like electronics, beauty, home and garden, and even sports and outdoor gear. 

Getting started is a breeze. You can either just ask ChatGPT a shopping question, and it'll suggest the research, or you can pick "shopping research" from the menu. Once you're in, you'll see a cool visual interface where you can chat and give feedback on product options, guiding the research as you go. ChatGPT will then search the internet for all the latest info like prices, availability, reviews, and specs, bringing those options back to you in real-time. 

What you end up with is a personalized buyer's guide, summarizing the best products, pointing out the key differences, and even highlighting any tradeoffs. It's like having a personal shopping assistant! While you can currently click through to retailer sites to buy, they're planning for direct purchases through ChatGPT in the future for some merchants. 

Under the hood, this is all powered by a special version of GPT-5 mini, trained specifically for shopping tasks. OpenAI is really emphasizing transparency here; your chats are never shared with retailers, and the results are organic, coming from public retail sites – they're even avoiding those spammy ones. They do want us to remember that while it's super accurate, it's not perfect, so it's always a good idea to double-check those details like price and availability on the merchant's site. Ultimately, they're aiming to make our shopping lives a whole lot simpler, and I think they're off to a fantastic start!
