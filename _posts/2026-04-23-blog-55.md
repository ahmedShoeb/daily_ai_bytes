## Key Points

- Qwen3.5-Omni is the latest advancement in the Qwen-Omni model family, representing significant evolution over its predecessor
- The model scales to hundreds of billions of parameters and supports a 256k context length
- It leverages massive datasets including heterogeneous text-vision pairs and over 100 million hours of audio-visual content
- Qwen3.5-Omni-plus achieves state-of-the-art results across 215 audio and audio-visual understanding, reasoning, and interaction subtasks and benchmarks
- The model surpasses Gemini-3.1 Pro in key audio tasks and matches it in comprehensive audio-visual understanding
- Architecture employs a Hybrid Attention Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) framework for both Thinker and Talker, enabling efficient long-sequence inference
- Supports over 10 hours of audio understanding and 400 seconds of 720P video processing
- Introduces ARIA (Audio Response Interleaving Alignment) to address streaming speech synthesis issues, enhancing stability and prosody
- Expands linguistic boundaries, supporting multilingual understanding and speech generation across 10 languages with emotional nuance
- Exhibits superior audio-visual grounding capabilities with script-level structured captions and automated scene segmentation
- Discovered new capability: Audio-Visual Vibe Coding - directly performing coding based on audio-visual instructions
