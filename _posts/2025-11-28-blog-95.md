## Key Points

Character.AI has launched "Stories," a new interactive fiction format for creating and sharing adventures.
Stories are designed for all users, particularly teens, offering a structured, visual, and multi-path narrative experience.
Users can select characters, a genre, and a premise, then make choices to guide the story's direction.
The feature includes replayable interactive fiction, simple creation tools, a visual-first format, and is user-generated content (UGC) centric.
This launch represents a strategic move to evolve Character.AI into a multimodal, AI-driven entertainment platform.

## Key Topics Discussed

Hey everyone! We've got some exciting news from Character.AI. They've just rolled out a brand-new feature called 'Stories,' and it sounds like a fantastic way to dive into interactive fiction. Imagine creating and sharing your own adventures starring your favorite Characters! This new format is designed for everyone, but it's especially geared towards teens, offering a structured, visual, and multi-path approach to storytelling that keeps the momentum going while letting you, the user, guide the narrative. Here’s how it works: you pick two or three Characters, choose a genre, and either write your own premise or let the tool auto-generate one for you. From there, you're on a guided narrative journey, making frequent choices that steer the plot and open up new possibilities. The coolest part? These Stories are completely replayable, with branching routes that make each run feel fresh and unique. Plus, they're shareable, so you can compare, discuss, and pass them around with your friends! This feature is packed with goodies, including replayable interactive fiction, super simple creation tools, a visual-first format, and it's all about user-generated content – meaning you can create your own Stories, build worlds, and share them with the entire community. Character.AI sees 'Stories' as a significant stride in their long-term vision to build the future of AI entertainment, moving beyond just open-ended chat to support new types of creativity through multimodal, AI-driven experiences. It really sounds like a safe and inspiring space for younger users and creators to express themselves. So, get ready to jump into 'Stories' and unleash your imagination!
