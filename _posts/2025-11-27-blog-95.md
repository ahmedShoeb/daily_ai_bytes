## Key Points

- LingGuang, Ant Group's AI coding assistant, achieved over 2 million downloads in six days.
- The app reached its first million downloads faster than OpenAI's ChatGPT and Sora.
- Its signature flash program tool, which allows users to create interactive apps with natural language prompts, briefly crashed due to overwhelming traffic.
- LingGuang is a multimodal AI tool, also offering 3D model generation, interactive charts, animations, and an 'AGI camera' for real-time analysis.
- The app is available internationally on various app stores and the web.
