## Key Points

Claude Cowork is expanding from desktop-only to web and mobile platforms, allowing work to follow users across all devices
Beta access is rolling out over several weeks starting with Max users, with more plans to follow
Cowork is an AI agent feature where users hand Claude tasks that work across files, calendar, email, messaging, and connected tools
Contrary to expectations, more than 90% of Cowork usage isn't software development - the largest categories are business operations and content creation
Typical tasks include reconciling quarterly spending, creating renewals trackers from contracts, and building client decks from transcripts and data
Three major improvements: 1) Work follows users between devices, 2) Work continues in background even without devices online, 3) Users stay in control with decision points reaching their phones
Scheduled tasks can now run automatically - users can set Monday's client prep for 6am and Claude will work through email threads, transcripts, and news
Web users can start Cowork sessions at claude.ai homepage, mobile users access via app sidebar, desktop remains the full experience
Chat and Cowork now share one home on web/desktop with projects and artifacts living together
Claude is extending doubled Cowork usage limits through August 5 to encourage trying bigger tasks
