## Key Points

- Coinbase launched 'Coinbase for Agents' - a new tool that enables AI agents to trade and transact directly on users' behalf
- Initially allows AI agents like ChatGPT or Claude to execute crypto trades using natural language instructions
- Capabilities include portfolio rebalancing, identifying trading opportunities, executing strategies, and managing positions
- Future expansion planned to include stocks and predictions beyond just crypto
- Uses Coinbase's machine-to-machine payments protocol called x402 for agentic payments
- x402 protocol enables agents to autonomously pay for digital services like paywalled research, data APIs, and compute resources
- More than 100 million transactions have occurred on the x402 protocol since its May 2025 launch
- Approximately 157,000 agents have acted as buyers using x402 in the past 30 days
- Coinbase earns trading fees on agent-executed trades and fees/spreads on USDC movement for payments
- Increased transaction volume benefits Base, Coinbase's Layer 2 blockchain
- Company sees agentic payments as a precursor to full agentic shopping experiences where agents make purchases autonomously
