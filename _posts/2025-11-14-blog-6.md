## Key Points

ElevenLabs has introduced Scribe v2 Realtime, a new low-latency Speech to Text model.
It delivers live transcription in under 150 milliseconds, setting a new standard for speed.
The model is specifically designed for live use cases, including voice agents, meeting assistants, and real-time captioning.
Scribe v2 Realtime supports English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, and Portuguese, along with 90 other languages.
It significantly outperforms other models in challenging conditions, such as samples with background noise and complex information.
The model achieves an impressive 93.5% accuracy across 30 commonly used European and Asian languages.
Scribe v2 Realtime is available immediately through the ElevenLabs API and directly within ElevenLabs Agents.
