## Key Points

- Google has made Gemini's Nano Banana-powered personalized image generation feature free for all eligible U.S. users
- This feature was previously only available to Plus, Pro, and Ultra subscribers
- Gemini can generate images based on users' interests and preferences without explicit prompts by leveraging Google account data
- The AI can access information from Gmail, Google Photos, YouTube, and Search to understand user preferences
- The feature allows users to simply request 'Create an illustration of me and my favorite things' without listing specifics
- Gemini can pull actual user photos from Google Photos automatically
- Personal Intelligence is an opt-in feature that users can enable or disable using a toggle in the Tools menu
- Google recently expanded this Personal Intelligence feature to users in India and Japan
- Gemini surpassed 750 million monthly active users earlier this year, solidifying its position in the AI market
