## Key Points

- Paper introduces Normalizing Trajectory Models (NTM) for few-step image generation
- NTM models each reverse step as a conditional normalizing flow with exact likelihood training
- Differs from existing methods like distillation, consistency training, or adversarial objectives that sacrifice likelihood framework
- Combines shallow invertible blocks within each step with deep parallel predictor across trajectory
- Architecture is end-to-end trainable from scratch or from pretrained flow-matching models
- Exact trajectory likelihood enables self-distillation for lightweight denoiser training
- Achieves high-quality samples in just four sampling steps
- On text-to-image benchmarks, matches or outperforms strong image generation baselines in four steps
- Uniquely retains exact likelihood over the generative trajectory
- Submitted on May 8, 2026, with revised version on May 12, 2026, by Ying Shen and colleagues
