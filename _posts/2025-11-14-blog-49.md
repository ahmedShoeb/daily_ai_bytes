## Key Points

OpenAI is developing a Group Chats feature for ChatGPT, enabling multiple users to participate in a shared conversation with both other users and the AI.
The feature is expected to include custom controls, such as the ability to customize system prompts for group discussions and manage when the AI responds (automatically or when tagged).
These controls suggest an emphasis on structured collaboration, targeting professional and academic users seeking tailored environments.
While similar to Microsoft Copilot's group features, OpenAI aims for more granular management over AI involvement and group behavior.
The release of Group Chats is anticipated around December, potentially coinciding with OpenAI's "12 days of OpenAI" event.
The feature is currently under development, with its full functionality and limitations yet to be confirmed.
