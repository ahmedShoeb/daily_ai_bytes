## Key Points

- AI-native startups operate with 40% smaller teams than non-AI SaaS companies
- AI startups raise larger funding rounds (30% higher Series A funding on average) while maintaining significantly smaller teams
- AI startups generate 6 times more revenue per employee compared to traditional startups ($3.48M vs $580K)
- Team sizes at AI startups reaching $10M in annual recurring revenue are estimated at 15-20 people vs 50-70 for traditional startups
- Venture funding for AI companies reached $211 billion in 2025, representing 85% year-over-year growth and roughly half of all global venture funding
- The startup economy is undergoing structural transformation with companies substituting compute for labor at an increasing rate
- Tech layoffs continued with over 126,000 workers cut at US tech companies in 2025, with the trend carrying into 2026
- Startup headcounts are generally shrinking across the board, with average seed round companies having under 3.5 employees in 2024 (down from 6.4 in 2022)
