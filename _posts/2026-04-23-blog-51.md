## Key Points

- OpenAI is reportedly planning a $7 trillion AI compute project called 'Stargate' starting in 2028
- Key sites shortlisted for the project include Iowa, Texas, Tennessee, Georgia, and Virginia
- Site selection is critical for strategic location near power grids, water resources, and transportation
- The $7 trillion investment dwarfs previous AI investments and equals roughly 20% of US GDP
- 'Stargate' refers to the final phase in a 5-phase AI infrastructure project with OpenAI and Microsoft
- Project aims to build enormous AI compute power to achieve Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)
- Defense implications are significant through OpenAI's dual-use work with the US government and military
- OpenAI is already working with the US Cyber Command and military contractors
- Stargate could give the US substantial AI and defense advantages against China
- The project faces challenges including financing, energy demands (50-105 gigawatts), and regulatory hurdles
