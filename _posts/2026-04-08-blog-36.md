## Key Points

- Anthropic has acquired stealth biotech AI startup Coefficient Bio in a $400 million stock deal, according to reports from The Information and Eric Newcomer
- Sources close to the deal confirmed it closed but declined to comment on the exact amount
- The acquisition is part of Anthropic's continued push into healthcare and life sciences, following their October announcement of Claude for Life Sciences
- Coefficient Bio was founded just eight months ago by Samuel Stanton and Nathan C. Frey, both former computational drug discovery researchers at Genentech's Prescient Design
- The startup was using AI to improve efficiency in drug discovery and other biological research
- The entire Coefficient Bio team, consisting of about 10 people, is expected to join Anthropic's health and life science division
