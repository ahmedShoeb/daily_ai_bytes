## Key Points

- OpenAI is updating ChatGPT's default model to GPT-5.5 Instant, making it smarter and more accurate for all users
- The new model provides 52.5% fewer hallucinated claims than GPT-5.3 Instant in critical domains like medicine, law, and finance
- It reduces inaccurate claims by 37.3% on conversations previously flagged for factual errors
- GPT-5.5 Instant offers tighter, more concise responses while maintaining the conversational warmth that makes ChatGPT enjoyable
- The model better utilizes context from past chats, files, and connected Gmail for more personalized responses
- OpenAI introduces 'memory sources' feature that shows users what context was used to personalize responses with new controls
- The model shows improvements in analyzing photo/image uploads, answering STEM questions, and determining when to use web search
- GPT-5.5 Instant is rolling out starting today, replacing GPT-5.3 Instant as the default model across all ChatGPT users
- For paid users, GPT-5.3 Instant will remain available for three months before being retired
- Enhanced personalization features are rolling out to Plus and Pro users first, with plans to expand to all tiers in coming weeks
