## Key Points

- Crusoe AI's managed inference service promises up to 9.9x faster time-to-first-token and up to 5x more tokens processed per second
- The technology uses proprietary MemoryAlloy technology, described as a 'unique cluster-native memory fabric' that enables persistent sessions and intelligent request routing
- Key benefits include eliminating latency, scaling to more users while maintaining consistent low latency, and reducing token spend while serving more users
- The platform allows users to bring their own models and aims to provide limitless throughput with resilient scaling
- Currently in development with limited model catalog availability, but promising expansion soon
