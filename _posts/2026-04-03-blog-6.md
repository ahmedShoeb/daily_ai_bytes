## Key Points

- Meta is introducing their first prescription-optimized AI glasses designed specifically for people who need vision correction
- Two new styles available: Blayzer (rectangular) and Scriber (rounded) frames starting at $499, available for pre-order
- The glasses feature optician-adjustable components for personalized fit, including overextension hinges and interchangeable nose pads
- New color combinations and lenses for Ray-Ban Meta and Oakley Meta frames, including Transitions® and Prizm™ lens technologies
- Advanced software updates include hands-free nutrition tracking where Meta AI extracts nutrition details from food photos
- WhatsApp summaries feature allows users to get concise group chat summaries through voice commands, processed on-device for privacy
- Neural handwriting feature enables writing replies with your finger on any surface, compatible with multiple messaging platforms
- Display recording combines in-lens display, world view, and audio into shareable videos
- Pedestrian navigation expanding to every US city in May, providing turn-by-turn directions in the lens
- Available starting today on Meta.com and Ray-Ban.com, with optical retailer availability beginning April 14
