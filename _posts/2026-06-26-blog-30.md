## Key Points

OpenAI is preparing to roll out a major upgrade to ChatGPT's voice mode called 'Bidi 1' (bidirectional voice model)
The new model allows ChatGPT to speak, hear, and listen simultaneously for more natural conversations
Bidi 1 features small natural acknowledgments like 'okay' or brief nods when users pause without interrupting
It can switch tasks on the fly - for example, if interrupted while counting to ten, it can immediately reverse direction
It maintains conversation context throughout entire discussions, unlike the current voice mode which drops earlier context
The model no longer jumps in during longer pauses, improving conversation flow
Creative capabilities like singing and beatboxing carry over from advanced voice mode, but copyright handling is tighter - it declines popular songs
The rollout appears to be gradual and opt-in across web and mobile platforms
European Economic Area users may face delays in getting access (not officially confirmed)
Codex is set for its own voice upgrade separately in the weeks following this launch
