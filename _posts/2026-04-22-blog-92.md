## Key Points

- Anthropic is launching Claude Design, a new product that allows users to collaborate with Claude AI to create visual designs, prototypes, slides, and marketing materials.
- Powered by the Claude Opus 4.7 vision model, it's available in research preview for Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise subscribers.
- The tool enables designers to explore widely and non-designers to produce visual work—users can describe what they need and Claude builds a first version.
- Features include importing from codebases/files, inline commenting, direct edits, custom sliders, and automatic application of team design systems.
- Use cases include realistic interactive prototypes, product wireframes, pitch decks, marketing collateral, and frontier design with code-powered prototypes.
- Integrates with Claude Code for implementation handoff and supports export to Canva, PDF, PPTX, and HTML files.
- Offers collaboration features with organization-scoped sharing and group conversations with Claude.
- Team testimonials highlight significant time savings—complex pages that took 20+ prompts in other tools required only 2 prompts in Claude Design.
- Early access includes companies like Canva and Brilliant, who praise its ability to streamline prototyping and maintain brand consistency.
- Enterprise organizations have Claude Design disabled by default, with admins able to enable it in Organization settings.
