## Key Points

- Google is building a dedicated inbox section inside the Gemini app for Business and Workspace customers
- The feature is currently in testing and not yet available to the public
- The layout includes three main filters: items to follow up on, what's been marked done, and work that needs review
- The system enables an Inbox Zero workflow that pulls messages from Gmail directly into Gemini rather than overlaying help on top of Gmail
- The "Needs review" filter suggests a proactive AI agent that triages email and files findings into structured to-do lists
- Google is transforming Gemini from a chatbot into a productivity worker with macOS apps, browser agents, and automation builders
- This move aligns with Google's strategy to create a consolidated desktop workspace within Gemini
- The feature follows similar patterns in other Google apps like Daily Brief, Gemini Spark, and the AI Inbox
