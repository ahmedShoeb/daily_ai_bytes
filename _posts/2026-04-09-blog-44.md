## Key Points

OpenAI sent a letter to California and Delaware attorneys general urging them to investigate Elon Musk's "improper and anti-competitive behavior"
OpenAI strategy chief Jason Kwon alleges Musk is working to undermine OpenAI by coordinating with Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg
Jury selection for Musk vs. OpenAI trial begins April 27 in Northern District of California
Kwon claims Musk's behavior could inhibit OpenAI's efforts to achieve artificial general intelligence (AGI)
OpenAI referenced a New Yorker report alleging Musk tracked Sam Altman's movements and circulated false allegations about the OpenAI CEO
OpenAI Chief Global Affairs Officer Chris Lehane questioned why two of the world's wealthiest people are trying to stop a nonprofit organization
OpenAI referenced SpaceX's IPO filing, suggesting Musk's legal efforts could benefit xAI's Grok platform
Musk has accused Delaware and California of bias against his companies, moving headquarters from California to Texas
xAI is suing California Attorney General Rob Bonta over state's AI data transparency law
