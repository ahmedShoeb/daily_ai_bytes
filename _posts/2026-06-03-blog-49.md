## Key Points

Introducing Bonsai Image 4B - a family of compact image-generation models designed to run high-quality diffusion inference on local hardware from laptops to phones.
Two variants available: 1-bit Bonsai Image 4B (binary {−1, +1} weights) for maximum compression, and Ternary Bonsai Image 4B ({−1, 0, +1} weights) for better visual quality while remaining compact.
First image model in its parameter class to run directly on an iPhone, providing 8.3x reduction in memory footprint for the 1-bit variant and 6.4x for the ternary variant.
Generates 512x512 images in 9.4 seconds on iPhone 17 Pro Max and about 6 seconds on Mac M4 Pro, up to 5.6x faster than stock full-precision pipeline.
Ternary variant retains 95% of FLUX.2 Klein 4B accuracy across benchmarks while reducing diffusion transformer footprint by 6.4x; 1-bit variant retains 88% accuracy with 8.3x reduction.
Bonsai Image 4B will be released with open weights and code under Apache 2.0 license, with an iOS app called Bonsai Studio for trying the model on iPhone.
Local image generation enables faster iteration, lower cost, and better privacy compared to cloud-only approaches, allowing image generation to sit directly inside product experiences.
Compression approach uses quantized transformer weights while keeping architecture intact, targeting the memory-intensive diffusion transformer component that runs repeatedly during generation.
