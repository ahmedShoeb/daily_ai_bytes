## Key Points

- Meta has launched Muse Image, its first image generation model from Meta Superintelligence Labs, now available in Meta AI
- Muse Image acts as a creative partner that transforms ideas into high-quality visuals that can be downloaded and shared across Meta's platforms
- The model powers over 30 new AI-powered effects for Instagram Stories and generates images in direct chats on WhatsApp
- Users can describe what they want in simple language and get outputs like restored photos, removal of photobombers, or custom QR codes
- The model renders text cleanly within images, enabling creation of how-to guides and detailed infographics
- Suggested presets help users get started with one-tap options like restoring old photos or reimagining themselves as different characters
- Users can take photos of rooms and ask Meta AI to redesign them with real products from the web or Facebook Marketplace
- Muse Image intelligently plans layouts, uses real-time web context, and blends multiple visual references through integration with Muse Spark
- Users can @-mention Instagram accounts to incorporate public photos from those profiles into generated images
- An editing feature allows users to circle, sketch, or annotate edits directly on photos without starting from scratch
