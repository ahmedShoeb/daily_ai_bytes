## Key Points

OpenAI has introduced GPT-5-Codex-Mini, a more compact version of GPT-5-Codex, offering approximately 4x more usage.
ChatGPT Plus, Business, and Edu users will now benefit from 50% higher rate limits.
ChatGPT Pro and Enterprise accounts receive priority processing for maximum speed.
Webhooks have been launched for API events such as completed responses, fine-tuning jobs, and batch jobs, recommended for long-horizon tasks.
A new Prompt object allows for central management, versioning, and optimization of prompts across various OpenAI platforms.
The Playground has been updated with an "Optimize" button for prompts and the ability to save, reuse, and share Prompts.
Evals now support tools and Structured Outputs, enabling evaluation of tool calls.
