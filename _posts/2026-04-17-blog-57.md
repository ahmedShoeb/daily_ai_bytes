## Key Points

MotherDuck introduces 'Dives' - a new way to connect your data warehouse to AI agents through MCP (Model Context Protocol)
The process involves four steps: connect your warehouse, ask questions in plain English via agents like Claude or ChatGPT, get interactive visualizations (Dives), and share or embed them
Dives eliminate the need for traditional BI tools and dashboards - AI agents write SQL and build live visualizations directly
Teams can share Dives with their team or embed them directly into products for customer-facing data experiences
The solution is already being used by teams at Together AI and other companies building next-generation data products
