## Key Points

Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite is Google's fastest and most cost-efficient Gemini 3 series model, designed for high-volume developer workloads at scale
The model is now available in preview to developers via Gemini API in Google AI Studio and for enterprises via Vertex AI
Priced at just $0.25/1M input tokens and $1.50/1M output tokens, offering enhanced performance at a fraction of the cost of larger models
Outperforms Gemini 2.5 Flash with 2.5X faster Time to First Answer Token and 45% increase in output speed while maintaining similar or better quality
Achieves an impressive Elo score of 1432 on the Arena.ai Leaderboard and excels in reasoning and multimodal understanding benchmarks
Comes standard with thinking levels in AI Studio and Vertex AI, allowing developers to control how much the model "thinks" for different tasks
Can handle complex workloads like generating user interfaces, creating simulations, dynamic weather dashboards, and analyzing large amounts of content
Early testers including Latitude, Cartwheel and Whering are already using 3.1 Flash-Lite to solve complex problems at scale
The model is designed for high-frequency workflows and real-time experiences where low latency is critical
Maintains adherence to instructions and can handle complex inputs with the precision of larger-tier models
