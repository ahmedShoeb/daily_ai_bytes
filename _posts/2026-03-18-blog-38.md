## Key Points

- Spatial-TTT is a framework for streaming visual-based spatial intelligence using Test-Time Training (TTT)
- The model updates spatial state with streaming video chunks then answers questions about spatial understanding
- Achieves state-of-the-art performance on video spatial benchmarks
- Uses a hybrid architecture that interleaves TTT layers with self-attention anchor layers
- Maintains adaptive fast weights that update online to accumulate 3D evidence from long video streams
- Employs sliding-window attention and spatial-predictive mechanisms with depthwise 3D convolutions
- Trained on Spatial-TTT-Data-97k dataset (available on Hugging Face)
- Implementation available for Qwen3-VL model fine-tuning
- Releases include Spatial-TTT-nano model trained on less than 1M samples
- Authored by researchers from Tsinghua University, Tencent Hunyuan, and NTU
