## Key Points

- 2026 predicted as the year of multi-agent systems and agent economy where AI agents delegate to subagents
- Major model launches expected: Claude 5/5.5, Gemini 3.5/4, GPT-5.3/6, DeepSeek-V4, Grok-5, Qwen-4, Kimi-K3, GLM-5, MiniMax M3
- Most frontier models will have 5-10T parameters, with possible 10T+ models for GPT-6 and Gemini-4
- Epoch Capabilities Index (>175) and 95%+ scores on several benchmarks predicted for frontier models
- Anthropic expected to lead, OpenAI/Google remain frontier labs, with potential for Anthropic to overtake OpenAI's valuation
- Release cycles accelerating from 6 months in 2023 to 3 months in 2024 to nearly monthly in 2026
- Model architecture improvements: more efficient/expressive architectures, hybrid architectures for longer context, looped language models
- AI video content (Veo-4, Sora-3) predicted to take off with consistent minute-long videos
- Full self-driving solved, 20%+ stock market drawdown predicted, and 15% chance of OpenAI going bankrupt
- Continual learning approached through various methods (self-meta-prompting, nested architectures, targeted training)
- LisanBench introduced as scalable benchmark testing knowledge, planning, constraints, and long-context reasoning
