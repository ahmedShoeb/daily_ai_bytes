## Key Points

- Comet Enterprise brings Perplexity's AI-powered browser to organizations with enterprise-grade security and controls
- Major companies like Fortune, AWS, AlixPartners, Gunderson Dettmer, and Bessemer Venture Partners are already using it
- The enterprise version features granular IT controls that let admins restrict AI actions to approved domains or limit to question-answering only
- Comet Enterprise integrates with CrowdStrike's Falcon platform for device-level security to block phishing, malware, and sensitive data sharing
- The solution includes silent deployment through existing MDM tools and comprehensive audit logs for visibility into AI usage
- Key use cases include customer meeting prep, document analysis, calculations, LinkedIn outreach, and industry research
- Users interact with Comet through a browser assistant button that accepts plain-language instructions
- Perplexity guarantees it never trains its models on enterprise data, ensuring data privacy
- Comet Enterprise is now available to all Perplexity Enterprise subscribers
